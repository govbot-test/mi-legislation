Section 1:
SUBSTITUTE FOR
A bill to amend 1931 PA 328, entitled
"The Michigan penal code,"
(MCL 750.1 to 750.568) by adding section 219g.
THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF MICHIGAN ENACT:
1 Sec. 219g. (1) A person who knowingly, intentionally, or
2 willfully engages in a practice or act that the person knows
3 provides false or misleading information related to an assisted
4 reproduction procedure, including by making a false or misleading
5 representation relating to any of the following, is guilty of a
6 felony punishable by imprisonment for not more than 5 years or a
7 fine of not more than $50,000.00, or both:
8 (a) The human embryo or gamete used or provided for assisted
9 reproduction.

2
1 (b) The identity of a donor of the human embryo or gamete used
2 or provided for assisted reproduction, including, but not limited
3 to, the donor's name, birth date, or address at the time of
4 donation.
5 (c) A donor's medical history, including present illness of
6 the donor at time of donation, any past illnesses of the donor, any
7 history of diagnosed mental illness, the social history of the
8 donor, any known genetic defects of the donor, the family medical
9 history of the donor, or the donor's level of education.
10 (2) A health professional who knowingly or recklessly uses or
11 provides a human embryo or gamete, including the health
12 professional's own human embryo or gamete, for assisted
13 reproduction other than the specific human embryo or gamete to
14 which the patient expressly consented in writing is guilty of a
15 felony punishable by imprisonment for not more than 15 years or a
16 fine of not more than $100,000.00, or both.
17 (3) The use of an anonymous donor, if requested by a patient,
18 is not a defense to a violation of this section committed by a
19 health professional.
20 (4) As used in this section:
21 (a) "Anonymous donor" means a donor whose identity is not
22 provided to the patient.
23 (b) "Assisted reproduction" means a method of causing
24 pregnancy other than sexual intercourse.
25 (c) "Donor" means an individual who provides gametes or human
26 embryos intended for use in assisted reproduction, regardless of
27 whether or not the individual receives financial compensation for
28 the donation.
29 (d) "Family medical history of the donor" means the complete
ELF H00179'25 (H-1) 5chd34
3
1 medical history of the first-degree, second-degree, and third-
2 degree relatives of the donor.
3 (e) "Gamete" means sperm, egg, or any part of a sperm or egg.
4 (f) "Health professional" means an individual licensed,
5 registered, certified, or otherwise authorized to engage in a
6 health profession under article 15 of the public health code, 1978
7 PA 368, MCL 333.16101 to 333.18838.
8 (g) "Human embryo" means a human organism at any stage of
9 development from fertilized ovum to embryo.
10 (h) "Identity" means legal name.
11 (i) "Know" or "knowingly" means that a health professional or
12 other person undertook an action with knowledge and not because of
13 mistake, accident, or other innocent reason.
14 (j) "Patient" means an individual under the care of a health
15 professional.
16 (k) "Social history of the donor" means the personal and
17 sexual history of the donor pertaining to risk factors for relevant
18 communicable disease transmissible via gamete donation.
19 Enacting section 1. This amendatory act takes effect 90 days
20 after the date it is enacted into law.
