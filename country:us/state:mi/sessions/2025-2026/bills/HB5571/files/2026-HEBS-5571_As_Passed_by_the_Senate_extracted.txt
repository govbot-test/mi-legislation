Section 1:
House BILL NO. 5571 Senate substitute for House BILL NO. 5571 A bill to amend 1972 PA 230, entitled "Stille-DeRossett-Hale single state construction code act," (MCL 125.1501 to 125.1531) by adding section 4k. the peoplE of the state of michigan enact: Sec. 4k. Section 4j does not apply if the department incorporates by reference the International Code Council's publication of single interior exit stairway for a multiple-family dwelling that is 4 or more levels above grade plane into the code. Enacting section 1. This amendatory act does not take effect unless Senate Bill No. ____ (request no. S00659'25) or House Bill No. 5570 of the 103rd Legislature is enacted into law.
