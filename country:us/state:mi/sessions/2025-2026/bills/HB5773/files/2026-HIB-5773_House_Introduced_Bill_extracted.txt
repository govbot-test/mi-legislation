Section 1:
HOUSE BILL NO. 5773 A bill to amend 1978 PA 368, entitled "Public health code," (MCL 333.1101 to 333.25211) by adding section 16282a. the people of the state of michigan enact: Sec. 16282a. A licensee or registrant shall refer a patient to a diagnostic center for fetal alcohol spectrum disorder that has been identified by the department of health and human services as a center of excellence if either of the following apply: (a) The patient is a minor who the licensee or registrant treats for a condition related to prenatal alcohol exposure. (b) The patient is a pregnant woman who the licensee knows or has reasonable cause to suspect is consuming alcohol.
