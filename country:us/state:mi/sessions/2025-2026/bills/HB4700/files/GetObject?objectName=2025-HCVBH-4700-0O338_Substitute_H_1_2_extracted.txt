Section 1:
SUBSTITUTE FOR
A bill to amend 1998 PA 58, entitled
"Michigan liquor control code of 1998,"
by amending section 107 (MCL 436.1107), as amended by 2021 PA 19.
THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF MICHIGAN ENACT:
1 Sec. 107. (1) "Cash" means money in hand, bank notes, demand
2 deposits at a bank, or legal tender, that a creditor must accept
3 according to law. Cash does not include call loans, postdated
4 checks, or promissory notes.
5 (2) "Class C license" means a place licensed to sell at retail
6 beer, wine, mixed spirit drink, and spirits for consumption on the
7 premises.
8 (2) "Church" means an entire house or structure set apart
9 primarily for use for purposes of public worship, that is tax

5 (3) "Class A hotel" means a hotel licensed by the commission
6 to sell beer, wine, and mixed spirit drink for consumption on the
7 premises only that provides for the rental of, and maintains the
8 availability for rental of, 20 or more rooms.
9 (4) "Class B hotel" means a hotel licensed by the commission
10 to sell beer, wine, mixed spirit drink, and spirits for consumption
11 on the premises only that provides for the rental of, and maintains
12 the availability for rental of, 20 or more rooms.
13 (5) "Class C license" means a place licensed to sell at retail
14 beer, wine, mixed spirit drink, and spirits for consumption on the
15 premises.
16 (6) (3) "Class G-1 license" means a place licensed to sell at
17 retail beer, wine, mixed spirit drink, and spirits for consumption
18 on the premises at a golf course having at least 18 holes that
19 measure at least 5,000 yards and which license is issued only to a
20 facility that permits member access by means of payments that
21 include annual paid membership fees.
22 (7) (4) "Class G-2 license" means a place licensed to sell at
23 retail beer, wine, and mixed spirit drink for consumption on the
24 premises at a golf course having at least 18 holes that measure at
25 least 5,000 yards and which license is issued only to a facility
26 that permits member access by means of payments that include annual
27 paid membership fees.
28 (8) (5) "Club" means a nonprofit association, whether
29 incorporated or unincorporated, organized for the promotion of some

1 common purpose, the object of which is owning, hiring, or leasing a
2 building, or space in a building, of an extent and character as in
3 the judgment of the commission may be suitable and adequate for the
4 reasonable and comfortable use and accommodation of its members and
5 their guests, but does not include an association organized for a
6 commercial or business purpose.
7 (9) (6) "Commission" means the liquor control commission
8 created in section 209.
9 (7) "Church" means an entire house or structure set apart
10 primarily for use for purposes of public worship, and that is tax
11 exempt under the laws of this state, and in which religious
12 services are held and with which a clergyman is associated, and the
13 entire structure of which is kept for that use and not put to any
14 other use inconsistent with that use.
15 (10) (8) "Distiller" means a person licensed to manufacture
16 and sell spirits or alcohol, or both, of any kind.
17 (11) "Financial records" means any document or summary of
18 information contained in a document, including electronic
19 documents, that contains information about the financial activities
20 or position of a person, including, but not limited to, information
21 about the person's assets, balance sheets, budgets, cash flow,
22 earnings, revenue, expenditures, income, investments, losses,
23 liabilities, payroll, profits, retained earnings, or taxes.
24 (12) (9) "Hotel" means a building or group of buildings
25 located on the same or adjoining pieces of real property, that
26 provide lodging to travelers and temporary residents and that may
27 also provide food service and other goods and services to
28 registered guests and to the public.

5 not less than 50 bedrooms if located in a local governmental unit

7 (11) "Class B hotel" means a hotel licensed by the commission

9 on the premises only, that provides for the rental of, and
10 maintains the availability for rental of, not less than 25 bedrooms
11 if located in a local governmental unit with a population of less
12 than 175,000 or not less than 50 bedrooms if located in a local
13 governmental unit with a population of 175,000 or more.
14 (12) "Financial records" means any document or summary of
15 information contained in a document, including electronic
16 documents, that contains information about the financial activities
17 or position of a person including, but not limited to, information
18 about the assets, balance sheets, budgets, cash flow, earnings,
19 revenue, expenditures, income, investments, losses, liabilities,
20 payroll, profits, retained earnings, or taxes.
21 (13) "License" means a contract between the commission and the
22 licensee granting authority to that licensee to manufacture and
23 sell, sell, or warehouse alcoholic liquor in the manner provided by
24 this act.
