ENROLLED HOUSE BILL No. 4303

AN ACT to designate the month of May of each year as Chaldean American Month.

The People of the State of Michigan enact:

Sec. 1. In recognition of Chaldean, Assyrian, and Syriac Americans, who have made significant and valuable contributions to this state and the United States, the legislature declares that the month of May of each year shall be known as "Chaldean American Month".

This act is ordered to take immediate effect.
