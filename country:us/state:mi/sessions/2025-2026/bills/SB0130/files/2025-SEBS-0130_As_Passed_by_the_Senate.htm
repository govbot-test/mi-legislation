(2) At the beginning of each state fiscal year, the department may increase the fees collected under subsection (1) by a percentage amount equal to not more than the average percentage wage and salary increase granted for that state fiscal year to classified civil service employees employed by the department.

(3) If the department increases fees under subsection (2), the increase is effective for that state fiscal year. The department shall use the increased fees as the basis for calculating fee increases in subsequent state fiscal years.

(4) By August 1 of each year, the department shall provide to the director of the department of technology, management, and budget and the chairpersons of the appropriations committees of the senate and house of representatives a complete schedule of fees to be collected under subsection (1) for the following state fiscal year.
