HOUSE BILL NO. 6030

A bill to amend 1956 PA 218, entitled

"The insurance code of 1956,"

(MCL 500.100 to 500.8302) by adding section 3406uu.

the people of the state of michigan enact:

Sec. 3406uu. (1) An insurer that delivers, issues for delivery, or renews in this state a group disability insurance policy shall do all of the following:

(a) Consider the closure of a health system or the removal of a health system from the insurer's network as a qualifying event for a special enrollment period.

(b) Allow an insured to terminate the group disability insurance policy without penalty if a health system in the insurer's network is closed or removed from the insurer's network.

(c) Offer a group disability insurance policy to an insured whose group disability policy with another insurer was affected by the closure or removal of a health system in the other insurer's network.

(d) If the insurer offers a group disability insurance policy under the circumstances described in subdivision (c), credit the insured any deductibles or out-of-pocket maximum that the insured paid under the other insurer's group disability insurance policy.

(2) As used in this section:

(a) "Health system" means an entity or affiliated group of entities under common ownership, governance, or control that operates or manages 1 or more hospitals and may also include physician practices, outpatient facilities, long-term care facilities, laboratories, and other health-care-related operations.

(b) "Qualifying event" means that term as described in 45 CFR 155.420.

(c) "Special enrollment period" means that term as described in 45 CFR 155.420.
