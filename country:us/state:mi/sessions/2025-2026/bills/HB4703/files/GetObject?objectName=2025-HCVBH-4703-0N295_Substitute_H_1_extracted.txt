Section 1:
SUBSTITUTE FOR
A bill to amend 1956 PA 218, entitled
"The insurance code of 1956,"
(MCL 500.100 to 500.8302) by adding section 3406ss.
THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF MICHIGAN ENACT:
1 Sec. 3406ss. An insurer that delivers, issues for delivery, or
2 renews in this state a health insurance policy shall provide
3 coverage for group prenatal care services. As used in this section:
4 (a) "Early childhood" means from birth until 12 months of age.
5 (b) "Group prenatal care services" means not more than 12
6 prenatal care visits provided in a group setting that are based on
7 an evidence-based model that may include health assessments, social
8 and clinical support, and educational activities in a family-
9 centered environment and peer-to-peer interaction that helps

2 into early childhood.
3 (c) "Group setting" means a group of not fewer than 3
