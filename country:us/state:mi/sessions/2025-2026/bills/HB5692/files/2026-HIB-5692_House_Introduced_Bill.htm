HOUSE BILL NO. 5692

A bill to amend 1956 PA 40, entitled

"The drain code of 1956,"

by amending sections 73 and 125 (MCL 280.73 and 280.125).

the people of the state of michigan enact:

Sec. 73. (1) The drain commissioner shall secure from a professional engineer, plans, specifications, and an estimate of cost a cost estimate of the proposed drain and descriptions of the lands or rights of way needed for the proposed drain.

(2) Beginning on the effective date of the amendatory act that added this subsection, not later than 7 days after the drain commissioner receives the plans, specifications, and cost estimate from the professional engineer under subsection (1), the drain commissioner shall send by first-class mail a letter containing a copy of the professional engineer's cost estimate to each freeholder in the drainage district.

(3) Not later than 21 days after a freeholder receives a letter from the drain commissioner as described under subsection (2), a freeholder may withdraw the freeholder's support of the petition underlying the proposed drain by notifying the drain commissioner in writing of the freeholder's withdrawal.

(4) If a sufficient number of freeholders withdraw support of the petition underlying the proposed drain so that the petition no longer has a level of support needed under section 71, another petition for the proposed drain must not be filed for at least 1 year after the filing date of the petition underlying the proposed drain.

(5) In approving the route of the drain proposed drain's route as furnished provided by the engineers professional engineer, the drain commissioner shall is not be limited to that the route described in the petition or in the first order of determination, if the a new route is more efficient and serviceable.

(6) The drain commissioner shall endeavor to secure from the owners of each parcel or tract of land to be traversed or damaged by the proposed drain or drains an easement or release of right of way and all related damages. on account thereof.
