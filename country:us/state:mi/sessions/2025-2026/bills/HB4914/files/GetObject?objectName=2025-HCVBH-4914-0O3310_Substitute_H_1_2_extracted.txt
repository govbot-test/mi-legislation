Section 1:
SUBSTITUTE FOR
A bill to amend 1980 PA 299, entitled
"Occupational code,"
by amending section 2010 (MCL 339.2010), as amended by 1992 PA 103.
THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF MICHIGAN ENACT:
1 Sec. 2010. (1) A firm may engage in the practice of
2 architecture, the practice of professional engineering, or the
3 practice of professional surveying in this state, if not less than
4 2/3 51% of the principals of the firm are licensees.
5 (2) However, a nonlicensed principal and the principal's firm
6 shall apply for and receive an approval from the department to
7 engage in the practice of architecture, the practice of
8 professional engineering, or the practice of professional
9 surveying, if the conduct of the firm and its the principals of the

2
1 firm comply with rules promulgated by the department.
2 (3) Upon On request by the department, a firm shall report to
3 the department the names and addresses of its the principals of the
4 firm, persons in responsible charge, unlicensed principals, and any
5 other information the department considers necessary.
6 (4) A firm shall employ a person in responsible charge in the
7 field of services offered at each place of business in this state
8 where services are offered by the firm, except at a field office
9 which that provides only a review of construction.
