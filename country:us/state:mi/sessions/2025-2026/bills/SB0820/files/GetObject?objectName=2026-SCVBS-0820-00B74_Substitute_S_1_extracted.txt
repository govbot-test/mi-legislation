Section 1:
SUBSTITUTE FOR
A bill to amend 1954 PA 116, entitled
"Michigan election law,"
by amending section 659 (MCL 168.659), as amended by 2014 PA 94.
THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF MICHIGAN ENACT:
1 Sec. 659. (1) If a county, city, ward, township, village,
2 metropolitan district, or school district is divided into 2 or more
3 election precincts, the county, city, ward, township, or village
4 election commissioners may, by resolution, consolidate the election
5 precincts for a particular election that is not a general November
6 election, primary election immediately before a general November
7 election, or other statewide or federal election. In making the
8 determination to consolidate election precincts for a particular
9 election, the election commission shall take into consideration the
STM S02905'25 (S-1) mkgfde
2
1 number of choices the voter elector must make, the percentage of
2 registered voters electors who voted at the last similar election
3 in the jurisdiction, the distance between election precinct polling
4 places, the access electors have to public transportation, and the
5 intensity of the interest of the electors in the jurisdiction
6 concerning the candidates and proposals to be voted upon. on.
7 Consolidated precincts shall must not exceed 5,000 15,000 active
8 registered electors.
9 (2) A consolidation under this section shall must be made not
10 less than 60 days before a primary, general, or special election.
11 (3) Unless the polling places for the election precincts to be
12 consolidated are located in the same building, when a county, city,
13 ward, township, or village consolidates election precincts for a
14 particular election under subsection (1), the election
15 commissioners or other designated election officials shall do both
16 of the following:
17 (a) Provide notice to the registered electors of the affected
18 election precincts of the consolidation of election precincts for
19 the particular election and the location of the polling place for
20 the election precinct or precincts for that election. Notice may be
21 provided by mail or other method designed to provide actual notice
22 to the registered electors.
23 (b) Post a written notice at each election precinct polling
24 place stating the location of the consolidated election precinct
25 polling place.
26 (4) If a county, city, ward, township, or village consolidates
27 election precincts under this section, each affected election
28 precinct shall must be treated as a whole unit and shall must not
29 be divided during the consolidation.
