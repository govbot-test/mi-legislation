Section 1:
SUBSTITUTE FOR
A bill to prohibit members of the legislature and certain
legislative staff from entering into certain nondisclosure
agreements.
THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF MICHIGAN ENACT:
1 Sec. 1. This act may be cited as the "nondisclosure agreement
2 act".
3 Sec. 2. (1) Beginning on and after the effective date of this
4 act, a member of the legislature or that member's legislative staff
5 shall not enter into a nondisclosure agreement regarding any work
6 done in that member's legislative capacity.
7 (2) A nondisclosure agreement entered into in violation of
8 this act is void.
9 Sec. 3. This act applies to any nondisclosure agreement that

3 Enacting section 1. This act does not take effect unless

5 (request no. H01306'25) of the 103rd Legislature is enacted into
6 law.
