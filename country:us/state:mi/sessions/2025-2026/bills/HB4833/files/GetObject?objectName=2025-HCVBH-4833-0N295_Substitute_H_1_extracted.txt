Section 1:
SUBSTITUTE FOR
A bill to amend 1961 PA 236, entitled
"Revised judicature act of 1961,"
by amending section 8123 (MCL 600.8123), as amended by 2018 PA 6.
THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF MICHIGAN ENACT:
1 Sec. 8123. (1) The forty-third district consists of the cities
2 of Madison Heights, Ferndale, and Hazel Park, is a district of the
3 third class, and has 3 judges.
4 (2) The forty-fourth district consists of the cities of Royal
5 Oak and Berkley and has 2 judges.
6 (3) The forty-fifth district consists of the cities of
7 Huntington Woods, Oak Park, and Pleasant Ridge and the township of
8 Royal Oak in the county of Oakland, is a district of the third
9 class, and has 2 judges.
SCS H04268'25 (H-1) hoye76
2
1 (4) The forty-sixth district consists of the cities of
2 Southfield and Lathrup Village and the township of Southfield in
3 the county of Oakland, is a district of the third class, and has 3
4 judges.
5 (5) The forty-seventh district consists of the cities of
6 Farmington and Farmington Hills, is a district of the third class,
7 and has 2 judges.
8 (6) The forty-eighth district consists of the cities of
9 Birmingham, Bloomfield Hills, Sylvan Lake, Keego Harbor, and
10 Orchard Lake Village and the townships of Bloomfield and West
11 Bloomfield in the county of Oakland, is a district of the third
12 class, and, subject to subsection (7), has the following number of
13 judges:
14 (a) Until the date determined under subdivision (b), the
15 forty-eighth district has 3 judges.
16 (b) The forty-eighth district has 2 judges beginning on the
17 earlier of the following dates:
18 (i) The date on which a vacancy occurs in the office of
19 district judge in this district, unless the vacancy occurs after
20 the vacating judge has been defeated in a primary or general
21 election.
22 (ii) The beginning date of the term for which an incumbent
23 district judge in this district no longer seeks election or
24 reelection to that office.
25 (7) If each district control unit of the forty-eighth district
26 approves the maintaining of 3 judges in the same manner as is
27 required for an additional district judgeship under section 8175(1)
28 and (2), subsection (6)(b) does not apply and section 8175(3)
29 applies to the maintaining of the 3 judges.
