Section 1:
SUBSTITUTE FOR
A bill to restrict certain immigration enforcement actions in
certain locations; and to provide for the powers and duties of
certain officers and entities.
THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF MICHIGAN ENACT:
1 Sec. 1. As used in this act:
2 (a) "Educational institution" means that term as defined in
3 section 2 of the Michigan election law, 1954 PA 116, MCL 168.2.
4 (b) "Federal immigration officer" means an officer, employee,
5 or individual paid by or acting as an agent of the United States
6 Immigration and Customs Enforcement or United States Customs and
7 Border Protection, or any division thereof, or any other officer,
8 employee, or individual paid by or acting as an agent of the United
9 States Department of Homeland Security.
LEP S01762'25 (S-3) i3gqv4
2
1 (c) "Immigration enforcement action" means any of the
2 following actions taken by a federal immigration officer for the
3 purpose of immigration enforcement or to obtain information in
4 regards to an individual's immigration or citizenship status:
5 (i) Arresting or interviewing an individual.
6 (ii) Conducting a search or surveillance.
7 (d) "Place of worship" means a church, synagogue, mosque, or
8 other institution of worship, including, but not limited to, a
9 building rented for a religious service.
10 (e) "Protected area" means all of the following:
11 (i) An educational institution.
12 (ii) A hospital or other health facility.
13 (iii) A courthouse.
14 (iv) The site of an organization that is assisting children,
15 pregnant women, victims of crime or abuse, or an individual with
16 significant mental or physical disabilities.
17 (v) A public library.
18 (vi) A place of worship.
19 Sec. 2. A federal immigration officer may not conduct an
20 immigration enforcement action in a nonpublic area of a protected
21 area, unless 1 or more of the following apply:
22 (a) The federal immigration officer presents a valid judicial
23 warrant.
24 (b) An exigent circumstance exists.
