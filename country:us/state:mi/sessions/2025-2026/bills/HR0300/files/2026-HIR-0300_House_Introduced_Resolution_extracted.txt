HOUSE RESOLUTION NO. 300 HOUSE RESOLUTION NO. 300 Reps. Weiss, Koleszar, Byrnes, Glanville, Wegela, McKinney, Price, B. Carter, Breen, Wilson, Mentzer, Foreman, Hoskins, Brixie, McFall, Steckloff and Rogers offered the following resolution: A resolution to declare May 4-8, 2026, as Teacher Appreciation Week in the state of Michigan. We encourage all residents to recognize and thank teachers for their dedication to students, schools, and communities.
