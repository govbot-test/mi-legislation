SUBSTITUTE FOR
A bill to amend 1941 PA 207, entitled
"Fire prevention code,"
by amending section 19 (MCL 29.19), as amended by 2024 PA 36.
THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF MICHIGAN ENACT:

2 of all schools, including state supported schools, and the owner,
3 or owner's representative, of all school dormitories shall have a

5 during school hours and when the school is open to the public. Each
6 teacher in a school, including a state supported school, and the
7 owner or owner's representative of a school dormitory shall comply
8 with these requirements and keep a record of the drills.
