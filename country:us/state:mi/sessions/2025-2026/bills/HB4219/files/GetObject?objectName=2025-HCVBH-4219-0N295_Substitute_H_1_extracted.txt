SUBSTITUTE FOR
A bill to amend 1974 PA 258, entitled
"Mental health code,"
by amending section 416 (MCL 330.1416), as amended by 2018 PA 595.
THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF MICHIGAN ENACT:
1 Sec. 416. Upon the commencement of mental health treatment ,
2 the rights that the patient has described in section 415, both of
3 the following must occur:
4 (a) A patient's rights during the mental health treatment,
5 including the right to object to the mental health treatment, must
6 be communicated orally communicated and in writing to the patient
7 and to the individual who executed the written consent. In
8 addition, a copy of the written consent A patient's rights include,
9 but are not limited to, the right to end voluntary mental health

1 treatment. The oral and written communication of a patient's rights

4 (b) Both of the following must be given to the patient, and

7 (i) A copy of the written consent.
