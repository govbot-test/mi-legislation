Section 1:
SUBSTITUTE FOR
A bill to create a Tricare premium reimbursement program; and
to provide for the powers and duties of certain state governmental
officers and entities.
THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF MICHIGAN ENACT:
1 Sec. 1. (1) This act may be cited as the "Tricare premium
2 reimbursement program act".
3 (2) As used in this act:
4 (a) "Department" means the department of military and veterans
5 affairs.
6 (b) "Eligible recipient" means a member of the Michigan
7 National Guard who is eligible for coverage under the Tricare
8 reserve select program or the Tricare dental program and meets any
9 other eligibility criteria established by the department.
JJR S00613'25 (S-1) 9j15tv
2
1 (c) "Fund" means the Michigan National Guard member benefit
2 fund created in the Michigan National Guard member benefit fund
3 act.
4 (d) "Program" means the Tricare premium reimbursement program
5 created in section 3.
6 (e) "Tricare dental program" means a voluntary dental health
7 insurance plan for eligible recipients who are not on active duty
8 and are not covered by a traditional assistance management program,
9 an existing health insurance program through an employer, or a
10 private market plan.
11 (f) "Tricare reserve select program" means a voluntary health
12 insurance plan for eligible recipients who are not on active duty
13 and are not covered by a traditional assistance management program,
14 an existing health insurance program through an employer, or a
15 private market plan.
16 Sec. 3. (1) The department shall create and operate a Tricare
17 premium reimbursement program. Under the program, the department
18 shall, if there is sufficient money in the fund to cover the
19 reimbursements, make reimbursements to eligible recipients who meet
20 the reimbursement application requirements for a premium paid for
21 either or both of the following:
22 (a) Individual coverage through the Tricare reserve select
23 program.
24 (b) Individual coverage under the Tricare dental program.
25 (2) To apply for reimbursement under the program, an eligible
26 recipient must submit an application to the department using a
27 form, as determined by the department, that contains the
28 information required by the department. The department shall make
29 the form described in this subsection, and the instructions for
JJR S00613'25 (S-1) 9j15tv
3
1 completing and submitting the form, available on the department's
2 website.
3 Sec. 5. The department shall promulgate rules to implement
4 this act under the administrative procedures act of 1969, 1969 PA
5 306, MCL 24.201 to 24.328.
6 Enacting section 1. This act does not take effect unless all
7 of the following bills of the 103rd Legislature are enacted into
8 law:
9 (a) Senate Bill No. 540.
10 (b) Senate Bill No. 542.
11 (c) House Bill No. 4962.
