HOUSE BILL NO. 4941

A bill to prohibit certain immigration enforcement agreements; and to provide for the powers and duties of certain state and local governmental officers and entities.

the people of the state of michigan enact:

Sec. 1. As used in this act:

(a) "Immigration enforcement agreement" means an agreement between a law enforcement agency and the Department of Homeland Security under section 287(g) of the immigration and nationality act, 8 USC 1357.

(b) "Law enforcement agency" means the department of state police, or a police agency of a city, village, township, or county.

Sec. 3. Beginning on the effective date of this act, a law enforcement agency shall not enter into, modify, or renew an immigration enforcement agreement.
