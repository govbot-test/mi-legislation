THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF MICHIGAN ENACT:
1 TITLE
2 An act to provide for certain requirements regarding suicide
3 prevention for schools; to prescribe the rights, powers, duties,
4 and privileges of schools, school districts, public school
5 academies, intermediate school districts, and other public school
6 entities, and nonpublic schools; to provide for and prescribe the
7 powers and duties of certain state departments; and to provide for
8 the regulation of certain school employees.

16 (b) "Nonpublic school" means that term as defined in section 5
17 of the revised school code, MCL 380.5.
18 (c) "OK2SAY program" means this state's student safety program
19 that allows students to confidentially report tips on potential
20 harm or criminal activities directed at students, school employees,
21 and schools.
22 (d) (b) "Public school" and "public school academy" mean those
23 terms as defined in section 5 of the revised school code, MCL
24 380.5.

26 section 6 of the revised school code, MCL 380.6.
27 (f) (d) "The revised school code" means the revised school
28 code, 1976 PA 451, MCL 380.1 to 380.1852.
