Sec. 11cc. In addition to the appropriations in section 11, for the fiscal year ending September 30, 2025, there is appropriated $100.00 from the state school aid fund for the public schools of this state and for certain other state purposes related to education.

Sec. 201i. For fiscal year 2023-2024 only, there is appropriated $3,300,000.00 from the coronavirus state fiscal recovery funds under the American rescue plan act of 2021, title IX, subtitle M of Public Law 117-2, to the Michigan Community College Association, for the community college academic catch-up program. Funds appropriated under this section must be used in accordance with the requirements described for the community college academic catch-up program established under former section 216a.

Sec. 236s. In addition to the appropriations in section 236, for the fiscal year ending September 30, 2025, there is appropriated $100.00 from the state general fund/general purpose money for purposes related to higher education.
