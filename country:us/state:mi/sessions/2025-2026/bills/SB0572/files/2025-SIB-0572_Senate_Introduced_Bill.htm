A bill to amend 1962 PA 199, entitled

"Ski area safety act of 1962,"

by amending sections 6 and 16 (MCL 408.326 and 408.336), section 6 as amended by 1981 PA 86.

the people of the state of michigan enact:

Sec. 6. (1) The board shall promulgate rules for the safe construction, installation, repair, use, operation, maintenance, and inspection of all ski areas and ski lifts as the board finds necessary for protection of the general public while using ski areas and ski lifts. The rules shall must be reasonable and based upon on generally accepted engineering standards, formulas, and practices.

(2) The board, with the advice of the commissioner, shall propose legislation to establish the fee schedule for permits, inspections, and plan review activities. The fees shall reflect the actual costs and expenses of the department for issuing permits and conducting inspections and plan reviews.
