(b) Replace lands converted or lost to other than public outdoor recreation use. (c) For parcels acquired that are larger than 5 acres, provide this state with a nonparticipating 1/6 minimum royalty interest in any acquired minerals that are retained by the grant recipient. (2) The agreements under subsection (1) must also provide that the full payments of grants be made only after proof of acquisition, or proof of completion of a development project, is submitted by the grant recipient and all costs are verified by the department of natural resources.
