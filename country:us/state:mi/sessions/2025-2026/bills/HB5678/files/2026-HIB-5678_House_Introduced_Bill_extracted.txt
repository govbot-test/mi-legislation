Section 1:
HOUSE BILL NO. 5678 A bill to amend 1978 PA 368, entitled "Public health code," (MCL 333.1101 to 333.25211) by adding section 21795a. the people of the state of michigan enact: Sec. 21795a. (1) The department, in consultation with the department of health and human services, shall establish and administer a fall prevention training program for unlicensed personnel working in a nursing home. (2) A nursing home shall ensure that unlicensed personnel working in the nursing home complete the fall prevention training program described in subsection (1).
