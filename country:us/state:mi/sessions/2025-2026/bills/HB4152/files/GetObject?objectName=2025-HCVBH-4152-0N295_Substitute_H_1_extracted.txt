SUBSTITUTE FOR
A bill to amend 1976 PA 451, entitled
"The revised school code,"
by amending section 1531e (MCL 380.1531e), as added by 2024 PA 147.
THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF MICHIGAN ENACT:
1 Sec. 1531e. (1) In addition to the requirements under sections
2 1538a and 1531i, as applicable, and subject to subsection (2), the
3 department shall not, beginning September 30, 2027, approve a
4 teacher preparation program or an alternative teaching program and
5 shall revoke, by September 30, 2027, shall revoke the approval of a
6 teacher preparation program or an alternative teaching program
7 unless the teacher preparation program or alternative teaching
8 program offers instruction regarding all of the following:
9 (a) The characteristics of dyslexia and underlying factors

6 can lead to social, emotional, and behavioral difficulties.
7 (c) Instructional adjustments for pupils with dyslexia and
8 instructional adjustments for addressing underlying factors that
9 place pupils at risk for difficulties in learning to decode
10 accurately and efficiently.
11 (d) Methods for developing schoolwide and classroom
12 infrastructure that meet the collective and individual needs of
13 pupils using a multi-tiered system of support (MTSS).
14 (e) For a teacher preparation program or an alternative

17 language arts, or special education, as appropriate, or for school

20 (i) Evidence-based instructional methods and features of

24 difficulties in learning to decode accurately and efficiently.
25 (ii) Evidence-based instructional methods and features of
26 evidence-based interventions that are grounded in the science of
27 reading and principles of structured literacy that are designed to
28 effectively meet the needs of most pupils.
