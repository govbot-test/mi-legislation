(2) Both of the following apply to an application for a building permit or an additional permit that an applicant submits to the department:

(a) If the department denies the application for a building permit or an additional permit, the department must notify the applicant of the basis for the denial. (b) If the department determines that the application for a building permit or an additional permit is not a complete application, the department must notify the applicant in writing or make the information electronically available to the applicant not later than 7 days after receipt of the application. (5) As used in this section:
