Section 1:
HOUSE BILL NO. 5231 A bill to designate the Honor and Remember flag as the official emblem of remembrance of the state of Michigan. the people of the state of michigan enact: Sec. 1. The Honor and Remember flag created by Honor and Remember, Inc., is designated as this state's official emblem of remembrance for those in the United States Armed Forces who have given their lives in the line of duty.
