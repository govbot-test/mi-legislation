ENROLLED SENATE BILL No. 274

AN ACT to amend 2001 PA 142, entitled An act to consolidate prior acts naming certain Michigan highways; to provide for the naming of certain highways; to prescribe certain duties of the state transportation department; and to repeal acts and parts of acts and certain resolutions, (MCL 250.1001 to 250.2092) by adding section 111.

The People of the State of Michigan enact:

Sec. 111. The portion of highway US-131 in Kalamazoo County between highway I-94 and Parkview Avenue shall be known as the Master Sergeant Gregory T. Kuhse Memorial Highway.

 

Enacting section 1. This amendatory act takes effect 90 days after the date it is enacted into law.

This act is ordered to take immediate effect.
