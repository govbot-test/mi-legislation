Section 1:
SUBSTITUTE FOR
A bill to amend 1976 PA 451, entitled
"The revised school code,"
(MCL 380.1 to 380.1852) by adding section 1280h.
THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF MICHIGAN ENACT:
1 Sec. 1280h. (1) By not later than July 1, 2026, the board of a
2 school district or an intermediate school district or the board of
3 directors of a public school academy shall post a link to the MI
4 School Data website's parent dashboard for school transparency for
5 each school operated by the school district, intermediate school
6 district, or public school academy on the school district's,
7 intermediate school district's, or public school academy's public
8 website.
9 (2) Subject to appropriation, by not later than July 1, 2027,
CRM H00223'25 (H-1) q1f9uo
2
1 and by July 1 of each year thereafter, a school district, an
2 intermediate school district, or a public school academy shall
3 annually notify the parent or legal guardian of each student
4 enrolled in a school operated by the school district, intermediate
5 school district, or public school academy. The annual notification
6 must include all of the following:
7 (a) Data for the school from the education dashboard of the MI
8 School Data website.
9 (b) The school's performance on state assessments by subject
10 area from the parent dashboard of the MI School Data website.
11 (3) CEPI shall expand the information included in the
12 education dashboard of the MI School Data website to include data
13 from the parent dashboard for school transparency of the MI School
14 Data website. CEPI shall ensure that the MI School Data website
15 includes a function to compare the information from the education
16 dashboard of the MI School Data website for the public schools in
17 this state. As used in this subsection, "CEPI" means the center for
18 educational performance and information created in section 94a of
19 the state school aid act of 1979, MCL 388.1694a.
