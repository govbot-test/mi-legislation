5 taxes under this act. This section does not apply to the property
6 of a corporation.

12 required for a person residing in the principal residence if that

16 not required to file a federal or state income tax return in the
17 tax year in which the exemption under this section is claimed or in

21 this subsection constitutes an appearance before the board of
