SUBSTITUTE FOR
A bill to amend 1933 PA 167, entitled
"General sales tax act,"
by amending section 25 (MCL 205.75), as amended by 2023 PA 20.
THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF MICHIGAN ENACT:
