HOUSE RESOLUTION NO. 308

Rep. Bollin offered the following resolution:

A resolution to declare May 2026 as Michigan Moves Month in the state of Michigan.

Whereas, Having the ability to confidently engage in a myriad of enjoyable physical activities is impactful in improving the health and wellbeing of all Michiganders; and

Whereas, Physical activity benefits physical health and improves the quality of life overall, and is a cost-effective, fun, safe, and evidence-based public health intervention to address chronic diseases, including diabetes, heart disease, and obesity, as well as mental illness; and

Whereas, The Governor's Council on Physical Fitness, Health and Sports was established in 1992 to encourage increased physical activity and improve the health of Michigan residents; and

Whereas, The Centers for Disease Control has recognized for decades the strong role parks and green spaces play in overall health within a community, and Michigan provides over one hundred public beaches, 103 state parks and recreation areas, nineteen million acres of forest, and thousands of miles of trails as places where people can be physically active; and

Whereas, Michigan is dedicated to improving the quality of life of all residents and visitors through movement to inspire and facilitate a culture of active living; and
