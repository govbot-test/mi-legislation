Section 1:
SUBSTITUTE FOR
A bill to amend 1939 PA 280, entitled
"The social welfare act,"
(MCL 400.1 to 400.119b) by adding section 109t.
THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF MICHIGAN ENACT:
1 Sec. 109t. Beginning on the effective date of the amendatory
2 act that added this section, the department shall provide coverage
3 under the medical assistance program for group prenatal care
4 services. As used in this section:
5 (a) "Early childhood" means from birth until 12 months of age.
6 (b) "Group prenatal care services" means not more than 12
7 prenatal care visits provided in a group setting that are based on
8 an evidence-based model that includes health assessments, social
9 and clinical support, and educational activities in a family-

2 pregnant individuals support one another during their pregnancy and
3 into early childhood.
4 (c) "Group setting" means a group of not less than 3
5 individuals.
