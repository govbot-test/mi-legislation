Section 1:
HOUSE BILL NO. 5812 A bill to amend 1939 PA 280, entitled "The social welfare act," (MCL 400.1 to 400.119b) by adding section 14o. the people of the state of michigan enact: Sec. 14o. (1) The department shall not seek, apply for, accept, or renew a waiver for work requirements under 7 USC 2015(o)(4) without first obtaining specific authorization from the legislature. (2) The department shall not exercise this state's option to provide exemptions from the work requirement under 7 USC 2015(o)(6)(F). This subsection must not be construed to limit the application of good cause exemptions for temporary absence from employment as provided in 7 CFR 273.24(b)(2).
