Section 1:
SENATE BILL NO. 907 SENATE BILL NO. 907 A bill to designate Eid al-Adha, Eid al-Fitr, and Eid al-Ghadeer as holidays in this state. the people of the state of michigan enact: Sec. 1. The legislature declares that the tenth day of the month of Dhul Hijjah, according to the Islamic Hijri calendar, shall be known as "Eid al-Adha". Sec. 2. The legislature declares that the first day of the month of Shawwal, according to the Islamic Hijri calendar, shall be known as "Eid al-Fitr". Sec. 3. The legislature declares that the eighteenth day of the month of Dhul Hijjah, according to the Islamic Hijri calendar, shall be known as "Eid al-Ghadeer".
