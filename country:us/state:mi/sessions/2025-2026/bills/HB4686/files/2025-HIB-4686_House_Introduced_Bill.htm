HOUSE BILL NO. 4686

A bill to amend 1978 PA 368, entitled

"Public health code,"

(MCL 333.1101 to 333.25211) by adding section 7404a.

the people of the state of michigan enact:

Sec. 7404a. (1) An individual who meets all of the following requirements is not in violation of section 7401, 7403, or 7404 for creating, manufacturing, possessing, or using psilocyn:

(a) The individual is 18 years of age or older.

(b) The individual possesses not more 2 ounces of a substance that contains psilocyn.

(c) The individual possesses the substance containing psilocyn only for personal use.

(d) The individual possesses a medical record that demonstrates the individual has a diagnosis of post-traumatic stress disorder.

(2) An individual who meets all of the following requirements is not in violation of section 7401, 7403, or 7404 for creating, manufacturing, possessing, or using psilocybin:

(a) The individual is 18 years of age or older.

(b) The individual possesses not more than 2 ounces of a substance that contains psilocybin.

(c) The individual possesses the substance containing psilocybin only for personal use.

(d) The individual possesses a medical record that demonstrates the individual has a diagnosis of post-traumatic stress disorder.
