andWHEREAS, For 75 years, MARSP has served as the collective voice of retired school employees including teachers, support staff, administrators, and other education professionals who have devoted their working lives to the success of Michigan芒聙聶s students and communities; andWHEREAS, MARSP has consistently advocated for the economic security, health, and dignity of retired school personnel, particularly through its leadership on retirement security, pension protections, health care access, and issues affecting the Michigan Public School Employees芒聙聶 Retirement System; andWHEREAS, Through legislative advocacy, member education, and community engagement, MARSP has helped ensure that the voices and lived experiences of retirees remain an important part of Michigan芒聙聶s public policy conversations; andWHEREAS, The 75th anniversary of MARSP provides a meaningful opportunity to recognize the lasting contributions of retired school personnel and to honor an organization that has supported them across generations; now, therefore, be itRESOLVED BY THE SENATE, That the members of this legislative body commemorate the 75th anniversary of the Michigan Association of Retired School Personnel (MARSP) and extend our appreciation for the decades of service, advocacy, and leadership on behalf of Michigan芒聙聶s retired school employees;
