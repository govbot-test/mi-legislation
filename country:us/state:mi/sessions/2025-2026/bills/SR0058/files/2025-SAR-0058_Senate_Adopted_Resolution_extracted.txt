SENATE RESOLUTION NO. 58 Senators Lindsey, Outman, Bayer, Damoose, Lauwers, Moss, Theis, Webber and Wojno offered the following resolution: A resolution to commemorate the 250th anniversary of the United States (U.S.) Army.
