(5) There is a rebuttable presumption of a violation of this section if an employer takes adverse personnel action against a person within 90 days after that person does any of the following:

(a) Files a complaint with the department or a court alleging a violation of this act.

(b) Informs any person about an employer's alleged violation of this act.

(c) Cooperates with the department or another person in the investigation or prosecution of any alleged violation of this act.

(d) Opposes any policy, practice, or act that is prohibited under this act.

(e) Informs any person of his or her rights under this act.
