HOUSE BILL NO. 5406

A bill to amend 1956 PA 218, entitled

"The insurance code of 1956,"

(MCL 500.100 to 500.8302) by adding section 3406ww.

the people of the state of michigan enact:

Sec. 3406ww. (1) An insurer that delivers, issues for delivery, or renews in this state a health insurance policy shall not provide coverage for a human organ transplant if either of the following applies:

(a) The transplant operation is performed in China or another country known to have participated in forced organ harvesting, as designated by the director of the department of health and human services.

(b) The human organ to be transplanted was procured by a sale or donation originating in China or another country known to have participated in forced organ harvesting, as designated by the director of the department of health and human services.

(2) As used in this section, "forced organ harvesting" means the removal of 1 or more organs from a living individual by means of coercion, abduction, deception, fraud, or abuse of power or a position of vulnerability.
