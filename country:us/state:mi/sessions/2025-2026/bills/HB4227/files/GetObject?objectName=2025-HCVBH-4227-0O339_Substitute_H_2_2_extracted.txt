SUBSTITUTE FOR
A bill to amend 1976 PA 451, entitled
"The revised school code,"
(MCL 380.1 to 380.1852) by adding section 1308g.
THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF MICHIGAN ENACT:
1 Sec. 1308g. Subject to appropriation, the board of each
2 intermediate school district shall designate at least 1 emergency
3 and safety manager and at least 1 mental health coordinator. All of
4 the following apply to the roles described in this section, as
5 applicable:
6 (a) An individual designated as an emergency and safety
7 manager shall do all of the following:
8 (i) Serve as a liaison between state agency school safety
9 offices, the emergency management and homeland security division

6 health commission created under section 6 of the comprehensive
7 school safety plan act, 2018 PA 548, MCL 28.806.
8 (ii) Work with school administrators, local and state emergency
9 management coordinators, and emergency responders to implement
10 prevention programs related to school safety; communicate with
11 parents, legal guardians, and the community about school safety;
12 and handle all safety-related issues concerning schools located in
13 the geographic boundaries of the intermediate school district.
14 (iii) Perform risk assessments of, and provide response training

18 to school safety plans, school safety trainings, school safety
