Section 1:
SUBSTITUTE FOR
A bill to amend 2012 PA 455, entitled
"Organized retail crime act,"
(MCL 752.1081 to 752.1087) by adding section 3a.
THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF MICHIGAN ENACT:
1 Sec. 3a. As used in section 4:
2 (a) "Gift card" means a physical or digital closed-loop gift
3 card or open-loop gift card that is either activated or
4 inactivated.
5 (b) "Gift card redemption information" means information
6 unique to each gift card that allows the cardholder to access,
7 transfer, or spend the funds on the gift card.
8 (c) "Issuer" means a person that issues a gift card or the
9 agent of the person with respect to that gift card.
ELF H03135'25 (H-1) 9cr1dm
2
1 (d) "Seller" means a merchant that is engaged in the business
2 of selling gift cards to consumers.
3 Enacting section 1. This amendatory act takes effect 90 days
4 after the date it is enacted into law.
5 Enacting section 2. This amendatory act does not take effect
6 unless House Bill No. 4598 of the 103rd Legislature is enacted into
7 law.
Final Page
