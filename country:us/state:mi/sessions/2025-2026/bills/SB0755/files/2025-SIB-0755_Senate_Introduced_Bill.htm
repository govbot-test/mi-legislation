Sec. 1507. Notwithstanding any other provision of this act, a conservation officer of the department shall not enter into or on private property unless 1 of the following applies:

(a) The owner or lessee of the property authorizes the entry.

(b) The conservation officer has a warrant issued by a court of competent jurisdiction authorizing the entry.

(c) The entry is to kill crippled or distressed wildlife that the conservation officer personally and lawfully observed on the private property.

(d) The entry is in response to an emergency situation, accident, or another threat to public safety that is occurring on the private property.
