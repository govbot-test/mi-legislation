Section 1:
SUBSTITUTE FOR
A bill to amend 1978 PA 368, entitled
"Public health code,"
by amending section 7204 (MCL 333.7204), as amended by 2012 PA 182.
THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF MICHIGAN ENACT:
1 Sec. 7204. If a substance is designated, rescheduled, or
2 deleted as a controlled substance under federal law and notice of
3 that designation, rescheduling, or deletion is given to the
4 administrator, the substance shall must be similarly scheduled
5 under section 7201 unless the administrator holds a board meeting
6 within the expiration of 91 not more than 60 days after notice is
7 received to determine whether the substance should be similarly
8 controlled under section 7201. If the administrator decides not to
9 similarly control schedule the substance, the administrator shall,

2
1 within 91 not more than 60 days after that decision is made,
2 publish the reasons for that determination.
