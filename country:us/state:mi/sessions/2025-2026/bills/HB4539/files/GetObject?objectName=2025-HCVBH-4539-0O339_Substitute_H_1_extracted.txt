1 transitional housing, and permanent housing.

3 aside at least 20% of the rental units included in the project for

5 (f) A home ownership project assisted by the fund must set
6 aside at least 20% of the housing units in the project for
7 households earning no more than 60% of the area median income.

14 the money had been allocated during that fiscal year, but shall
15 must be reallocated for the next fiscal year according to the next
16 fiscal year's allocation plan.
17 (4) (5) Prior to Before developing the biennial allocation
18 plan, the authority shall hold public hearings in at least 3
19 separate locations in this state regarding the content of the
20 biennial allocation plan. The authority must provide an option for
21 virtual participation by members of the public in all public
22 meetings and employ additional methods to gather public comment.
23 The alternative methods must focus on engagement with persons with
24 disabilities, people with limited English proficiency, and people
25 from low-income, very low-income, extremely low-income, or middle-
26 income households. The authority may make modifications to the
27 allocation plan necessary to facilitate the administration of the
28 Michigan housing and community development program or to address
29 unforeseen circumstances.
