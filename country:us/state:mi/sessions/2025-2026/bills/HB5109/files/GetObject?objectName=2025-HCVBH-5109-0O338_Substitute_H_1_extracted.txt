A bill to amend 1949 PA 300, entitled
"Michigan vehicle code,"
by amending section 810b (MCL 257.810b), as amended by 2009 PA 99.
THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF MICHIGAN ENACT:
1 Sec. 810b. (1) The transportation administration collection
2 fund is created within the state treasury.
3 (2) The Except as otherwise provided in subsection (9), the
4 state treasurer may receive money from the collections authorized
5 under this act for deposit into the fund. The state treasurer shall
6 direct the investment of the fund. The state treasurer shall credit
7 to the fund interest and earnings from fund investments.
8 (3) Money in the fund at the close of the fiscal year shall
9 not lapse into the Michigan transportation fund.
