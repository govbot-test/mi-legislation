Section 1:
HOUSE BILL NO. 4476 A bill to designate May 8 of each year as Victory in Europe Day. the people of the state of michigan enact: Sec. 1. To commemorate the victory of the United States and its allies over Nazi Germany in the second world war, the legislature declares that May 8 of each year shall be known as "Victory in Europe Day" in this state.
