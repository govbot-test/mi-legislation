SUBSTITUTE FOR
A bill to amend 1994 PA 451, entitled
"Natural resources and environmental protection act,"
by amending section 3122 (MCL 324.3122), as amended by 2023 PA 140.
THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF MICHIGAN ENACT:
1 Sec. 3122. (1) Until October 1, 2027, the department may levy
2 and collect an annual groundwater discharge permit fee from
3 facilities or municipalities that discharge wastewater to the
4 ground or groundwater of this state under section 3112. The fee is
5 as follows:
6 (a) For a group 1 facility, $7,500.00.
7 (b) For a any of the following, $1,800.00:
8 (i) A group 2 facility. or a
9 (ii) A mobile home park licensed under section 16 of the mobile

5 act, 1987 PA 96, MCL 125.2302.
6 (iii) A campground licensed under part 125 of the public health
7 code, 1978 PA 368, MCL 333.12501 to 333.12546, that has 1,000 or
8 fewer sites and that would otherwise be considered a group 1
9 facility. As used in this subparagraph, "campground" means that
10 term as defined in section 12501 of the public health code, 1978 PA
11 368, MCL 333.12501.
12 (iv) A municipality of 1,000 or fewer residents. , $1,800.00.
13 (c) For a group 2a facility, $300.00.
14 (d) For a group 3 facility, $240.00.
15 (2) Within Not later than 180 days after receipt of a complete
16 application for a permit to discharge wastewater to the ground or
17 to groundwater, the department shall grant or deny a permit, unless
18 the applicant and the department agree to extend this time period.
19 If the department fails to make a decision on an application within
20 the time period specified or agreed to under this subsection, an

22 shall receive a 15% annual discount on the annual groundwater
23 discharge permit fee.
24 (3) If the person required to pay the annual groundwater
25 discharge permit fee under subsection (1) is a municipality, the
26 municipality may pass on the annual groundwater discharge permit
27 fee to each user of the municipal facility.
