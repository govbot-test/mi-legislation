Section 1:
SUBSTITUTE FOR
A bill to amend 1978 PA 368, entitled
"Public health code,"
by amending section 16653 (MCL 333.16653), as added by 2018 PA 463,
and by adding section 16650.
THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF MICHIGAN ENACT:
1 Sec. 16650. (1) Beginning 12 months after the effective date
2 of the amendatory act that added this section, if a licensee is
3 required by rule to complete continuing education courses approved
4 by the board as a condition of license renewal, the board shall
5 consider as approved any continuing education course that meets all
6 of the following requirements:
7 (a) The course covers a subject required by rule.
8 (b) The course is offered by a nonprofit dental care
EMR H03374'25 (H-1) mdovha
2
1 corporation operating under 1963 PA 125, MCL 550.351 to 550.373,
2 that is domiciled in this state, that administers dental benefits
3 under Medicaid, and that is the largest provider of dental benefits
4 in this state measured by the number of covered individuals.
5 (2) As used in this section, "Medicaid" means the program of
6 medical assistance established under section 105 of the social
7 welfare act, 1939 PA 280, MCL 400.105.
8 Sec. 16653. As Subject to section 16650, as a condition of
9 renewal of a license to practice under sections 16651 to 16658, a
10 dental therapist shall certify that he or she the dental therapist
11 has successfully completed 35 hours of continuing education in the
12 2 years before renewal. Continuing education under this section
13 must conform with the requirements of part 161 concerning
14 continuing education courses and must include board-approved
15 courses, including, but not limited to, a course in cardiopulmonary
16 resuscitation.
