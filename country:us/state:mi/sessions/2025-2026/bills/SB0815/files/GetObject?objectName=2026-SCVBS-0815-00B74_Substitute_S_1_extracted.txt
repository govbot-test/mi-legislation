Section 1:
SUBSTITUTE FOR
A bill to amend 2023 PA 282, entitled
"Candidate for office financial disclosure act,"
by amending section 5 (MCL 169.305), as amended by 2025 PA 4.
THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF MICHIGAN ENACT:
1 Sec. 5. (1) Except as otherwise provided in this section, a
2 candidate for office shall file a financial disclosure report with
3 the department by not later than May 15 of each year in which there
4 is an election involving a candidate for office, or if the
5 candidate for office files a statement of organization for that
6 candidate's candidate committee after May 15 in order to be
7 nominated by a political party at the political party's nominating
8 convention, the report required to be filed under this subsection
9 must be filed no later than 15 days after that candidate files the

2
1 statement of organization for that candidate's candidate committee.
2 (2) Beginning after May 15, 2027, a candidate for office shall
3 file a financial disclosure report with the department by not later
4 than April 15 of each year in which there is an election involving
5 a candidate for office, or if the candidate for office files a
6 statement of organization for that candidate's candidate committee
7 after April 15 in order to be nominated by a political party at the
8 political party's nominating convention, the report required to be
9 filed under this subsection must be filed no later than 15 days
10 after that candidate files the statement of organization for that
11 candidate's candidate committee.
12 (3) (2) If a candidate for office who is required to file a
13 report under this act receives notice from the secretary of state
14 under section 13(1)(h), the candidate for office shall, not later
15 than 9 business days after receiving the notice, file corrections
16 to the errors or omissions or file the report, as applicable.
17 (3) A candidate for office shall file the financial disclosure
18 report for the reporting period of January 1, 2024 to December 31,
19 2024 not later than June 13, 2025.
20 (4) If a candidate for office is not elected, that candidate
21 for office is not required to file the report required under this
22 section for any year after the year in which the candidate for
23 office ran for that public office unless that candidate runs again
24 as a candidate for office.
25 (5) If a candidate for office is required to report under the
26 public officers financial disclosure act, 2023 PA 281, MCL 15.701
27 to 15.715, the candidate for office is not required to file a
28 report under this act.
