HOUSE BILL NO. 5724

A bill to amend 1939 PA 288, entitled

"Probate code of 1939,"

(MCL 710.21 to 712B.41) by adding section 18u to chapter XIIA.

the people of the state of michigan enact:

CHAPTER XIIA

Sec. 18u. If a court-ordered treatment plan includes referring a child to placement in a residential treatment program, the department shall ensure that the child is placed with a residential treatment program not more than 14 days after the referral.
