Section 1:
SUBSTITUTE FOR
A bill to amend 2016 PA 436, entitled
"Unmanned aircraft systems act,"
(MCL 259.301 to 259.331) by adding section 8.
THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF MICHIGAN ENACT:
1 Sec. 8. (1) Subject to subsection (3), a police officer may
2 intercept, disable, or destroy an unmanned aircraft that is flying
3 in a manner that poses a risk to public safety or that is flying in
4 violation of federal law or Federal Aviation Administration
5 guidelines.
6 (2) A police officer who intercepts, disables, or destroys an
7 unmanned aircraft under subsection (1) is not liable to the owner
8 of the unmanned aircraft for damage to the unmanned aircraft.
9 (3) Subsection (1) applies only to a police officer who

2
1 completes federally approved training and certification procedures
2 for exercising the authority described in subsection (1).
3 (4) This section applies only to the extent that this section
4 is not preempted by federal law.
