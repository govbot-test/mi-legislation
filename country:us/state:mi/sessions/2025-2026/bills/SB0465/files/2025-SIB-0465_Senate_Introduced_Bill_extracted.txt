Section 1:
SENATE BILL NO. 465 A bill to amend 1915 PA 31, entitled "Youth tobacco act," (MCL 722.641 to 722.645) by adding section 1m. the people of the state of michigan enact: Sec. 1m. (1) A person is exempt from the licensure provisions of this act as provided for in subsection (2) if the person files an affidavit with the department that affirms all of the following: (a) Before the effective date of the amendatory act that added this section, the person lawfully sold a nicotine or tobacco product at retail. (b) An application under section 1e has been submitted for the person and the application is pending department approval. (c) The person has not been granted a license. (2) A person's exemption under subsection (1) expires when the person's application under section 1e is no longer pending department approval. Enacting section 1. This amendatory act does not take effect unless all of the following bills of the 103rd Legislature are enacted into law: (a) Senate Bill No. ____ (request no. S01093'25) or House Bill No. ____ (request no. H01093'25). (b) Senate Bill No. 463. (c) Senate Bill No. 466. (d) Senate Bill No. 464.
