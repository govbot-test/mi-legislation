Section 1:
SUBSTITUTE FOR
A bill to amend 1915 PA 31, entitled
"Youth tobacco act,"
(MCL 722.641 to 722.645) by adding section 1i.
THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF MICHIGAN ENACT:
1 Sec. 1i. (1) A person is exempt from the licensure provisions
2 of this act as provided for in subsection (2) if the person files
3 an affidavit with the department that affirms all of the following:
4 (a) Before the effective date of the amendatory act that added
5 this section, the person lawfully sold a nicotine or tobacco
6 product at retail.
7 (b) An application under section 1c has been submitted for the
8 person and the application is pending department approval.
9 (c) The person has not been granted a license.

2
1 (2) A person's exemption under subsection (1) expires when the
2 person's application under section 1c is no longer pending
3 department approval.
4 Enacting section 1. This amendatory act does not take effect
5 unless all of the following bills of the 103rd Legislature are
6 enacted into law:
7 (a) Senate Bill No. 462 or House Bill No. ____ (request no.
8 H01093'25).
9 (b) Senate Bill No. 463 or House Bill No. ____ (request no.
10 H01094'25).
11 (c) Senate Bill No. 466 or House Bill No. ____ (request no.
12 H01094'25 a).
