Section 1:
HOUSE BILL NO. 5316 A bill to amend 1961 PA 236, entitled "Revised judicature act of 1961," by amending section 1631 (MCL 600.1631). the people of the state of michigan enact: Sec. 1631. The county in which the seat of state government is located is a proper county in which to commence and try the following actions: (a) when the action is commenced by the attorney general in the name of the state or of the people of the state for the use and benefit thereof; (b) when an action if venue cannot be laid under any other of the venue provisions.provision.
