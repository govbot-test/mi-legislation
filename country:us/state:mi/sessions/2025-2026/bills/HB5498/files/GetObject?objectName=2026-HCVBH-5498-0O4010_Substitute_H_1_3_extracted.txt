Section 1:
SUBSTITUTE FOR
A bill to require certain agencies to designate certain
employees to certain positions to manage the issuance of permits.
THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF MICHIGAN ENACT:
1 Sec. 1. As used in this act:
2 (a) "Agency" means a department of this state.
3 (b) "Permit" means any agency permit, license, certificate,
4 approval, registration, or similar form of permission required by
5 law or agency rule.
6 Sec. 3. (1) If an agency issues permits under state law or
7 rule, the agency must do both of the following:
8 (a) Separate the permits into subject areas based on the
9 industry that the permits regulate.
10 (b) For each subject area, assign an employee to manage the

1 issuance of permits for that subject area.
2 (2) The subject area that the agency assigns to an employee
3 under subsection (1) is the employee's primary subject area.
4 However, an employee may assist another employee in issuing
5 permits for a subject area that is not the employee's primary
6 subject area.
