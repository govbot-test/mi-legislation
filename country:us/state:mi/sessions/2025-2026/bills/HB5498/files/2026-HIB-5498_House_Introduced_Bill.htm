HOUSE BILL NO. 5498

A bill to require certain agencies to designate certain employees to certain positions to manage the issuance of permits.

the people of the state of michigan enact:

Sec. 1. As used in this act:

(a) "Agency" means a department of this state.

(b) "Permit" means any agency permit, license, certificate, approval, registration, or similar form of permission required by law or agency rule.

Sec. 3. (1) If an agency issues permits under state law or rule, the agency must do all of the following:

(a) Separate the permits into subject areas based on the industry that the permits regulate.

(b) Subject to subsection (2), designate employees to each of the subject areas to manage the issuance of permits.

(2) An employee who manages the issuance of permits for an agency must be designated to only 1 subject area.
