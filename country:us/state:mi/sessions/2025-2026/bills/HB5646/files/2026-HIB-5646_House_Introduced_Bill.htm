HOUSE BILL NO. 5646

A bill to amend 1976 PA 451, entitled

"The revised school code,"

(MCL 380.1 to 380.1852) by adding section 1531k.

the people of the state of michigan enact:

Sec. 1531k. (1) In addition to the requirements under section 1538a, as applicable, the department shall not, beginning September 30, 2027, approve a teacher preparation program or an alternative teaching program and, by September 30, 2027, shall revoke the approval of a teacher preparation program or an alternative teaching program unless the teacher preparation program or alternative teaching program does all of the following:

(a) Requires, as a condition for graduation, completion of LETRS training by all individuals participating in the teacher preparation program or alternative teaching program.

(b) Requires completion of LETRS training by all individuals who instruct courses or programs in literacy or reading methods in the teacher preparation program or alternative teaching program.

(c) Requires, as a condition for graduation, comprehensive knowledge of the science of reading by all individuals participating in the teacher preparation program or alternative teaching program.

(d) Requires comprehensive knowledge of the science of reading by all individuals who instruct courses or programs in literacy or reading methods in the teacher preparation program or alternative teaching program.

(2) As used in this section:

(a) "LETRS" means that term as used in section 35a of the state school aid act of 1979, MCL 388.1635a.

(b) "Science of reading" means that term as defined in section 1280f.
