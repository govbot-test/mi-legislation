Section 1:
SUBSTITUTE FOR
A bill to amend 1976 PA 451, entitled
"The revised school code,"
(MCL 380.1 to 380.1852) by adding section 1531l.
THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF MICHIGAN ENACT:
1 Sec. 1531l. (1) Beginning September 30, 2027, the
2 superintendent of public instruction shall issue a teaching
3 certificate or applicable endorsement under section 1531 only to an
4 individual who has completed a teacher preparation program that
5 does both of the following:
6 (a) Requires completion of the content described in subsection
7 (2) by all individuals who instruct courses or programs in literacy
8 or reading methods in the teacher preparation program.
9 (b) Requires comprehensive knowledge of the science of reading

1 by all individuals who instruct courses or programs in literacy or
2 reading methods in the teacher preparation program.
3 (2) By not later than September 30, 2027, an institution of
4 higher education or an alternative teaching program as described in
5 section 1531i that operates a teacher preparation program shall
6 ensure that the teacher preparation program meets both of the
7 following content requirements:
8 (a) Teacher content knowledge and pedagogical knowledge of the
9 critical components of literacy, including how the brain learns to
10 read, phonological and phonemic awareness, letter knowledge,
11 phonics, advanced phonics, vocabulary and oral language, fluency,
12 comprehension, spelling and writing, and the organization of
13 language as appropriate for the grade level and content area in
14 which a teacher candidate seeks certification and endorsement.
15 (b) Understanding how to effectively use screening, progress
16 monitoring, and diagnostic assessment data to improve literacy
17 outcomes through prevention and intervention for reading
18 difficulties in a multi-tiered system of supports, as appropriate
19 for the grade level and content area in which a teacher candidate
20 seeks certification and endorsement. The multi-tiered system of
21 supports must include at least all of the following essential
22 components:
23 (i) Team-based leadership.
24 (ii) A tiered delivery system.
25 (iii) Selection and implementation of instruction,
26 interventions, and supports.
27 (iv) A comprehensive screening and assessment system.
28 (v) Continuous data-based decision making.
29 (3) A teacher candidate may satisfy the requirements of this
