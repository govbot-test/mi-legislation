Section 1:
SUBSTITUTE FOR
A bill to provide for the establishment of a state-based
health insurance exchange as a nonprofit corporation; to create the
board of exchange and prescribe its powers and duties; to provide
for assessments and user fees; and to provide for the powers and
duties of certain state and local governmental officers and
agencies.
