HOUSE BILL NO. 4882

A bill to repeal 1964 PA 158, entitled

"An act to provide for the licensing of wholesale potato dealers; to prescribe certain powers and duties for certain state agencies; to require certain types of financial security for certain persons under certain circumstances, and to prescribe the procedure for its enforcement; to provide remedies and penalties for violations of the act; and to repeal certain acts and parts of acts,"

(MCL 290.451 to 290.466).

the people of the state of michigan enact:

Enacting section 1. 1964 PA 158, MCL 290.451 to 290.466, is repealed.
