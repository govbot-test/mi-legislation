Sec. 1135. (1) Upon enrollment of a student pupil for the first time in a local school district, a public school academy, or an intermediate school district, or the Michigan School for the Deaf, the local school district, the public school academy, or the intermediate school district, or the Michigan School for the Deaf shall notify in writing the person individual enrolling the student pupil that, within 30 days, he or she shall the individual must provide to the local school district, the public school academy, or the intermediate school district, or the Michigan School for the Deaf either of the following:

(a) A copy of the student's pupil's birth certificate.

(b) Other reliable proof, as determined by the local school district, public school academy, or intermediate school district, or the Michigan School for the Deaf, of the student's pupil's identity and age, and an affidavit explaining the inability to produce a copy of the birth certificate.

(2) If a person an individual enrolling a student pupil fails to comply with subsection (1), the local school district, public school academy, or intermediate school district, or the Michigan School for the Deaf shall notify the person individual enrolling the student pupil in writing that, unless he or she the individual complies within 30 days after the notification, the case must be referred to the local law enforcement agency for investigation.
