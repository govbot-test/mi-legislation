SUBSTITUTE FOR
A bill to amend 1976 PA 451, entitled
"The revised school code,"
by amending section 1135 (MCL 380.1135), as amended by 2018 PA 619.
THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF MICHIGAN ENACT:
1 Sec. 1135. (1) Upon enrollment of a student pupil for the
2 first time in a local school district, a public school academy, or
3 an intermediate school district, or the Michigan School for the
4 Deaf, the local school district, the public school academy, or the
5 intermediate school district, or the Michigan School for the Deaf
6 shall notify in writing the person individual enrolling the student
7 pupil that, within 30 days, he or she shall the individual must
8 provide to the local school district, the public school academy, or
9 the intermediate school district, or the Michigan School for the

1 Deaf either of the following:
2 (a) A copy of the student's pupil's birth certificate.
3 (b) Other reliable proof, as determined by the local school
4 district, public school academy, or intermediate school district,
5 or the Michigan School for the Deaf, of the student's pupil's
6 identity and age, and an affidavit explaining the inability to
7 produce a copy of the birth certificate.
8 (2) If a person an individual enrolling a student pupil fails
9 to comply with subsection (1), the local school district, public
10 school academy, or intermediate school district, or the Michigan
11 School for the Deaf shall notify the person individual enrolling
12 the student pupil in writing that, unless he or she the individual
13 complies within 30 days after the notification, the case must be
14 referred to the local law enforcement agency for investigation. If
15 the person individual enrolling the student pupil fails to comply
16 within that 30-day period, the local school district, public school
17 academy, or intermediate school district, or the Michigan School
18 for the Deaf shall notify the local law enforcement agency.
19 (3) The local school district, public school academy, or
20 intermediate school district, or the Michigan School for the Deaf
21 shall immediately report to the local law enforcement agency any
22 affidavit received under this section that appears inaccurate or
23 suspicious in form or content.

2 (b) The pupil's date of birth.
3 (c) The grade level in which the pupil will be enrolled at the
4 receiving school.
5 (d) The name and address of the receiving school.
6 (e) The date that the pupil officially enrolled in the
7 receiving school.
8 (5) Subject to subsection (6), any school that compiles
9 records for each student pupil in the school and that is requested
10 to forward a copy of a transferring student's pupil's record to the
11 new school under subsection (4) shall comply within 30 days after
12 receipt of the request unless the record has been tagged under
13 section 1134. If a student record has been tagged under section
14 1134, a copy of the student record must not be forwarded, and the
15 requested school shall notify the law enforcement agency that
16 notified the school district of the missing student under section 8
17 of 1968 PA 319, MCL 28.258, of the request.
18 (6) A school shall not provide a copy of a pupil's student
19 record under subsection (5) unless the school has been provided
20 with written confirmation from the school that will be receiving
21 the pupil that the pupil has been enrolled in the school as
22 described in subsection (4).
23 (7) (5) A local school district, a public school academy, or
24 an intermediate school district, or the Michigan School for the
25 Deaf shall not disclose any personally identifiable information
26 contained in a student record to a law enforcement agency, except
27 in compliance with 20 USC 1232g, commonly referred to as the family
28 educational rights and privacy act.
