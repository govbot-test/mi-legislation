Section 1:
HOUSE BILL NO. 5636 HOUSE BILL NO. 5636 A bill to amend 2001 PA 142, entitled "Michigan memorial highway act," (MCL 250.1001 to 250.2092) by adding section 123. the people of the state of michigan enact: Sec. 123. The portion of highway US-24, also known as Telegraph Road, between 9 Mile Road and 14 Mile Road shall be known as the "Deputy Claude Wilson Memorial Highway".
