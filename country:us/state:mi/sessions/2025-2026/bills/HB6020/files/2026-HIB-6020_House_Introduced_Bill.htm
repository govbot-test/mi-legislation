(9) As used in this section and sections 2222a and 2222b:

Sec. 2222a. (1) If ibogaine is approved by the United States Food and Drug Administration to treat a medical condition, only a physician may prescribe ibogaine for a patient.

(2) A physician who prescribes ibogaine for a patient under subsection (1) shall supervise the administration of ibogaine at a hospital or other licensed health facility to ensure the patient's safety while the patient is under the influence of ibogaine.

(3) The administration of ibogaine under this section must comply with rules promulgated under section 2222.
