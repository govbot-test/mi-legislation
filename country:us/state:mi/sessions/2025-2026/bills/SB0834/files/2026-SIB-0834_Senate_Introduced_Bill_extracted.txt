Section 1:
SENATE BILL NO. 834 A bill to amend 1972 PA 106, entitled "Highway advertising act of 1972," by amending section 17b (MCL 252.317b), as added by 2014 PA 2. the people of the state of michigan enact: Sec. 17b. (1) The owner of a sign, the agent of the owner of a sign, or a property owner or the agent of a property owner with whom the sign owner has a contractual relationship to maintain the sign on his or her that property shall not cross a limited access right-of-way to erect or maintain a sign without a permit issued under subsection (3). (2) The Except as otherwise provided in subsection (3), the department shall not issue a permit to cross a limited access right-of-way for purposes of erecting or maintaining a sign to the owner of a sign, the agent of the owner of a sign, or a property owner or the agent of a property owner with whom the sign owner has a contractual relationship to maintain the sign on his or her that property. (3) The department shall issue a permit to cross a limited access right-of-way for the purposes of erecting or maintaining a sign to the owner of a sign, the agent of the owner of a sign, or a property owner or the agent of a property owner with whom the sign owner has a contractual relationship to maintain the sign on that property if the property is only accessible by crossing the limited access right-of-way. (4) (3) If the owner of a sign, the agent of the owner of a sign, or a property owner or the agent of a property owner with whom the sign owner has a contractual relationship to maintain the sign on his or her that property accesses a sign by crossing a limited access right-of-way to erect or maintain the sign without a permit issued under subsection (3), the owner of the sign is subject to the following penalties: (a) For the first violation, a fine of $1,000.00 for each sign location. (b) For a second violation, removal of the sign and sign structure and cancellation of the permit associated with the sign.
