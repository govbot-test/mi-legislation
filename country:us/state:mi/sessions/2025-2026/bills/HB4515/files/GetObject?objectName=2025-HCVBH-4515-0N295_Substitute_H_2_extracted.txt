Section 1:
SUBSTITUTE FOR
A bill to amend 1939 PA 280, entitled
"The social welfare act,"
(MCL 400.1 to 400.119b) by adding section 14k.
THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF MICHIGAN ENACT:
1 Sec. 14k. (1) The digital photographic image and signature of
2 a recipient of food assistance or cash assistance benefits must be
3 captured or reproduced upon issuance of a Michigan bridge card. The
4 department shall acquire equipment purchased or leased under this
5 section under standard purchasing procedures of the department of
6 technology, management, and budget based on standards and
7 specifications established by the department. A digital
8 photographic image and signature captured under this section must
9 appear on the recipient's Michigan bridge card. If more than 1

2
1 individual is authorized by the department to use the Michigan
2 bridge card, the digital photographic image and signature of all
3 individuals over the age of 18 authorized to use the card must
4 appear on the card.
5 (2) As used in this section, "Michigan bridge card" means a
6 debit card issued by the department to deliver food assistance or
7 cash assistance benefits to the recipient through an electronic
8 benefits transfer.
