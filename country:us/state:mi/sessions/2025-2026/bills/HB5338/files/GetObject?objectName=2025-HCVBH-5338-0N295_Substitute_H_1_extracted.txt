Section 1:
SUBSTITUTE FOR
A bill to amend 1976 PA 451, entitled
"The revised school code,"
(MCL 380.1 to 380.1852) by adding section 1170c.
THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF MICHIGAN ENACT:
1 Sec. 1170c. (1) By not later than January 1, 2027, the
2 department, in consultation with this state's federally designated
3 organ procurement organization, may develop or adopt, and make
4 available to public schools, a model presentation on organ, eye,
5 and tissue donation and transplantation programs. The model
6 presentation must not exceed 1 class period and must include
7 information about the Michigan organ donor registry.
8 (2) Beginning in the 2027-2028 school year, the board of a
9 school district or intermediate school district or board of
CRM H05569'25 (H-1) 9up128
2
1 directors of a public school academy is encouraged to provide the
2 model presentation described in subsection (1) to students before
3 completion of grade 9.
4 (3) Upon the written request of a student's parent or legal
5 guardian, the student must be excused, without penalty, from
6 attending the model presentation described in subsection (1).
