SENATE BILL NO. 466

A bill to amend 1971 PA 79, entitled

"Age of majority act of 1971,"

by amending sections 2 and 3 (MCL 722.52 and 722.53), section 2 as amended by 1990 PA 104 and section 3 as amended by 2022 PA 170.

the people of the state of michigan enact:

Sec. 2. (1) Except as otherwise provided in the state constitution of 1963, and subsection (2), and section 3, notwithstanding any other provision of law to the contrary, a person who is at least and beginning January 1, 1972, an individual 18 years of age on or after January 1, 1972, or older is an adult of legal age for all purposes whatsoever, and shall have and has the same duties, liabilities, responsibilities, rights, and legal capacity as persons heretofore acquired at an individual 21 years of age before January 1, 1972.

(2) A court may order support payments for a person an individual 18 years of age or older as provided in 1 or more of the following:

(a) Chapter 84 of the revised statutes of 1846, being sections 552.1 to 552.45 of the Michigan Compiled Laws.1846 RS 84, MCL 552.1 to 552.45.

(b) The child custody act of 1970, Act No. 91 of the Public Acts of 1970, being sections 722.21 to 722.29 of the Michigan Compiled Laws.1970 PA 91, MCL 722.21 to 722.31.

(c) The family support act, Act No. 138 of the Public Acts of 1966, being sections 552.451 to 552.459 of the Michigan Compiled Laws.1966 PA 138, MCL 552.451 to 552.459.

(d) The paternity act, Act No. 205 of the Public Acts of 1956, being sections 722.711 to 722.730 of the Michigan Compiled Laws.1956 PA 205, MCL 722.711 to 722.730.

(e) Act No. 293 of the Public Acts of 1968, being sections 722.1 to 722.6 of the Michigan Compiled Laws.1968 PA 293, MCL 722.1 to 722.6.
