(3) The report under subsection (2) must include the following statements made by the department: (a) A statement that confirms the department reviewed and affirmed compliance by each grant recipient to program scope and expenditure of grant funding. (b) A statement that confirms the department reviewed applicable program reports and requests for reimbursement. Sec. 228.
