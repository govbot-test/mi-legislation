Section 1:
SENATE CONCURRENT RESOLUTION NO. 11 Senator Singh offered the following concurrent resolution: A concurrent resolution to provide for the final adjournment of the Legislature. Resolved by the Senate (the House of Representatives concurring), That when the Senate adjourns on December 18, 2025, it stands adjourned until December 23, 2025, at 11:30 a.m.; and be it further Resolved, That when the House of Representatives adjourns on December 18, 2025, it stands adjourned until December 23, 2025, at 11:30 a.m.; and be it further Resolved, That when the Legislature adjourns on December 23, 2025, it stands adjourned without day.
