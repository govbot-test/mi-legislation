SUBSTITUTE FOR
A bill to amend 2003 PA 215, entitled
"Credit union act,"
by amending section 387 (MCL 490.387).
THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF MICHIGAN ENACT:
1 Sec. 387. (1) A domestic credit union that is not a corporate
2 credit union shall apply for and maintain insurance of member its
3 share accounts and member deposit accounts from an agency of the
4 federal government that provides that insurance.a federal agency
5 authorized to insure share and deposit accounts or from a qualified
6 private insurance organization.
7 (2) A credit union that is denied a commitment for insurance
8 of its share and deposit accounts by the an insuring federal agency
9 or qualified private insurance organization, or that is given

7 director not later than 30 days after the denial or notice for
8 additional time to obtain an insurance commitment.
9 (3) The commissioner director shall grant a credit union
10 described in subsection (2) 1 or more additional periods of time to
11 obtain or reinstate an insurance commitment if the commissioner
12 director receives satisfactory evidence that the credit union has
13 made or is making a substantial effort to meet the conditions

15 insurance organization for issuance of an insurance commitment.
16 (4) A credit union may contract with an insurance carrier
17 licensed to do business in this state for insurance of the balances
18 of its accounts that exceed the amount insured by the an insuring

3 (7) A credit union that converts to primary share or deposit

5 comply with state and federal law applicable to the conversion and
6 must provide notice to the credit union's members as required under
7 section 43 of the federal deposit insurance act, 12 USC 1831t, and
8 12 CFR part 708b.
9 (8) As used in this section:
10 (a) "Primary share or deposit insurance" means insurance of a
