Section 1:
SUBSTITUTE FOR
A bill to amend 1976 PA 451, entitled
"The revised school code,"
(MCL 380.1 to 380.1852) by adding section 1138a.
THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF MICHIGAN ENACT:
1 Sec. 1138a. (1) The governing body of a public school shall
2 consider a pupil's absence from school to be an excused absence if
3 both of the following apply:
4 (a) The pupil is absent from school to attend a scheduled
5 competition, exhibition, or event offered by any of the following
6 organizations:
7 (i) The National FFA Organization.
8 (ii) The Michigan FFA association.
9 (iii) A 4-H club.
CRM H01929'25 (H-1) oiudxa
2
1 (iv) Any other agricultural organization approved by the board
2 of the school district in which the pupil's school is located.
3 (b) The pupil is in good academic standing, as determined by
4 the governing body of the pupil's school.
5 (2) This section does not permit a pupil to exceed the number
6 of excused absences allowed by the pupil's school district.
7 (3) The board of a school district or intermediate school
8 district or board of directors of a public school academy shall
9 ensure that its attendance policy is consistent with this section.
