Section 1:
HOUSE CONCURRENT RESOLUTION NO. 4 Rep. Posthumus offered the following concurrent resolution: A concurrent resolution providing for the final adjournment of the Legislature. Resolved by the House of Representatives (the Senate concurring), That when the House of Representatives adjourns on Thursday, December 18, 2025, it stands adjourned until Tuesday, December 23, 2025, at 11:30 a.m.; and be it further Resolved, That when the Senate adjourns on Thursday, December 18, 2025, it stands adjourned until Tuesday, December 23, 2025, at 11:30 a.m.; and be it further Resolved, That when the Legislature adjourns on Tuesday, December 23, 2025, it stands adjourned without day.
