SUBSTITUTE FOR
A bill to amend 1994 PA 451, entitled
"Natural resources and environmental protection act,"
by amending sections 47328 and 47330 (MCL 324.47328 and 324.47330),
as added by 1995 PA 57.
THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF MICHIGAN ENACT:
1 Sec. 47328. A person shall not use any kind of a boat, tug, or
2 launch, except when used in hook and line fishing, or any kind of
3 net or nets, set hook lines, or commercial trolling rigs for the
4 purpose of taking, catching, killing, or transporting fish in any
5 of the waters bordering on this state, regardless of whether for
6 commercial purposes or for personal use, without first having
7 applied for and been issued a license for that activity, in
8 accordance with this part. A license, except as otherwise provided

5 except as otherwise provided by law, is not required of persons

16 using more than a single crew consisting of not more than 4 people

18 prior to before the date of application for a license, any person

20 record at a port outside of the state, or any firm, company,
21 copartnership, partnership, association, or corporation in which
22 any of their stock, boats, nets, and fishing equipment has been
