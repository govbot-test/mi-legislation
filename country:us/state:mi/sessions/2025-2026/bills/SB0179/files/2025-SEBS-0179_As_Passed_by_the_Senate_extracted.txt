(ii) Decline to execute the grant agreement and lapse the associated funds at the end of the fiscal year. (f) At any point during the fiscal year, legislative grant sponsors may be added to a grant request. (b) Menstruation and menopause support, including, but not limited to, counseling or medical resources, symptoms training, and insurance coverage. (c) Fair and transparent pay practices, including, but not limited to, salary disclosures, clear criteria for raises, and employer tools to assess compensation and address wage disparities.
