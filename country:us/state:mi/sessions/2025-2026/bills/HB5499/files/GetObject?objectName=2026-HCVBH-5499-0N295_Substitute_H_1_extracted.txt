Section 1:
SUBSTITUTE FOR
A bill to amend 1969 PA 306, entitled
"Administrative procedures act of 1969,"
by amending section 101 (MCL 24.301).
THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF MICHIGAN ENACT:
1 Sec. 101. (1) When a person has exhausted all administrative
2 remedies available within an agency, and is aggrieved by a final
3 decision or order in a contested case, whether such the final
4 decision or order is affirmative or negative in form, the final
5 decision or order is subject to direct review by the courts as
6 provided by law. Exhaustion of administrative remedies does not
7 require the filing of a motion or application for rehearing or
8 reconsideration unless the agency rules require the filing before
9 judicial review is sought. A preliminary, procedural or
RMH H04500'25 (H-1) siuay8
2
1 intermediate agency action or ruling is not immediately reviewable,
2 except that the court may grant leave for review of such the action
3 if review of the agency's final decision or order would not provide
4 an adequate remedy.
5 (2) When a person is denied a permit by an agency of this
6 state, and after that person has exhausted all administrative
7 remedies available within an agency, the person may bring a civil
8 action against the agency that denied the permit. The court shall
9 award monetary damages and reasonable attorney fees only if the
10 person that brings the civil action prevails.
