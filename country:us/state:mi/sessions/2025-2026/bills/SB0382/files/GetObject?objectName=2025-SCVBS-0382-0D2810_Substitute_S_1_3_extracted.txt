Section 1:
SUBSTITUTE FOR
A bill to create a financial aid program for certain residents
of this state who attend certain postsecondary educational
institutions in this state; to provide for the administration of
the financial aid program; to provide for the promulgation of
rules; and to provide for the powers and duties of certain state
officers and entities.
THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF MICHIGAN ENACT:
1 Sec. 1. (1) This act may be cited as the "Michigan achievement
2 scholarship act".
3 (2) The Michigan achievement scholarship is created in this
4 act and, subject to appropriation, is funded in article III of the
5 state school aid act of 1979, 1979 PA 94, MCL 388.1836 to 388.1891,
6 to provide last-dollar scholarship assistance to certain residents

8 state's evolving economy and workforce.

29 313, MCL 390.991.
