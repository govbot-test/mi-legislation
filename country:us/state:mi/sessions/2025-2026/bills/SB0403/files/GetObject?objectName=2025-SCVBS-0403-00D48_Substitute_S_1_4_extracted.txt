Section 1:
SUBSTITUTE FOR
A bill to amend 1978 PA 368, entitled
"Public health code,"
(MCL 333.1101 to 333.25211) by adding section 7333c.
THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF MICHIGAN ENACT:
1 Sec. 7333c. (1) A pharmacist who receives a lawful order from
2 a prescriber for addiction medication shall make a good-faith
3 effort to fill the order without undue delay. If the pharmacy does
4 not have the addiction medication in stock, the pharmacist shall
5 offer to do 1 or more of the following for the patient:
6 (a) Obtain the addiction medication using the pharmacy's
7 standard expedited ordering procedure.
8 (b) Locate a pharmacy that is reasonably accessible to the
9 patient that has the addiction medication in stock and transfer the
EMR S00527'25 (S-1) 2csreb
2
1 order to that pharmacy using the transferring pharmacy's standard
2 transfer procedure.
3 (2) If an addiction medication is prescribed to a patient on a
4 prescription form and is not filled at the pharmacy, the pharmacist
5 shall return the prescription form to the patient on the patient's
6 request.
7 (3) As used in this section:
8 (a) "Addiction medication" means a controlled substance to
9 treat or manage an opioid use disorder.
10 (b) "Undue delay" means a delay intended to obstruct a
11 patient's access to the addiction medication or that is not a valid
12 delay.
13 (c) "Valid delay" means a delay caused by fulfilling the
14 requirements of this article, part 177, or a rule promulgated under
15 this article or part 177, or any other delay commonly associated
16 with the practice of pharmacy.
