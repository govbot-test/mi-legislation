Section 1:
SENATE BILL NO. 403 A bill to amend 1978 PA 368, entitled "Public health code," (MCL 333.1101 to 333.25211) by adding section 7333c. the people of the state of michigan enact: Sec. 7333c. (1) A pharmacist who receives a lawful order from a prescriber for addiction medication shall dispense the addiction medication without undue delay to the ultimate user. (2) A pharmacy shall have not less than a 2-week supply of addiction medication in stock at all times in the pharmacy. (3) As used in this section: (a) "Addiction medication" means a controlled substance approved by the United States Food and Drug Administration for the treatment of an opioid use disorder, including, but not limited to, buprenorphine. (b) "Undue delay" means a delay intended to obstruct a patient's access to the addiction medication or that is not a valid delay. (c) "Valid delay" means a delay caused by fulfilling the requirements of this article, part 177, or a rule promulgated under this article or part 177, or any other delay commonly associated with the practice of pharmacy.
