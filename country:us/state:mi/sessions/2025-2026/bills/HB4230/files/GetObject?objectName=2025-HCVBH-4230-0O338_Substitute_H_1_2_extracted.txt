Section 1:
SUBSTITUTE FOR
A bill to amend 1951 PA 51, entitled
"An act to provide for the classification of all public roads,
streets, and highways in this state, and for the revision of that
classification and for additions to and deletions from each
classification; to set up and establish the Michigan transportation
fund; to provide for the deposits in the Michigan transportation
fund of specific taxes on motor vehicles and motor vehicle fuels;
to provide for the allocation of funds from the Michigan
transportation fund and the use and administration of the fund for
transportation purposes; to promote safe and efficient travel for
motor vehicle drivers, bicyclists, pedestrians, and other legal
users of roads, streets, and highways; to set up and establish the
truck safety fund; to provide for the allocation of funds from the
truck safety fund and administration of the fund for truck safety
purposes; to set up and establish the Michigan truck safety
commission; to establish certain standards for road contracts for
certain businesses; to provide for the continuing review of
transportation needs within the state; to authorize the state
transportation commission, counties, cities, and villages to borrow

money, issue bonds, and make pledges of funds for transportation
purposes; to authorize counties to advance funds for the payment of
deficiencies necessary for the payment of bonds issued under this
act; to provide for the limitations, payment, retirement, and
security of the bonds and pledges; to provide for appropriations
and tax levies by counties and townships for county roads; to
authorize contributions by townships for county roads; to provide
for the establishment and administration of the state trunk line
fund, local bridge fund, comprehensive transportation fund, and
certain other funds; to provide for the deposits in the state trunk
line fund, critical bridge fund, comprehensive transportation fund,
and certain other funds of money raised by specific taxes and fees;
to provide for definitions of public transportation functions and
criteria; to define the purposes for which Michigan transportation
funds may be allocated; to provide for Michigan transportation fund
grants; to provide for review and approval of transportation
programs; to provide for submission of annual legislative requests
and reports; to provide for the establishment and functions of
certain advisory entities; to provide for conditions for grants; to
provide for the issuance of bonds and notes for transportation
purposes; to provide for the powers and duties of certain state and
local agencies and officials; to provide for the making of loans
for transportation purposes by the state transportation department
and for the receipt and repayment by local units and agencies of
those loans from certain specified sources; to investigate and
study the tolling of roads, streets, highways, or bridges; and to
repeal acts and parts of acts,"
(MCL 247.651 to 247.675) by adding section 13c.

6 the fund and credit interest and earnings from the investments to
7 the fund.
8 (3) Money in the fund at the close of the fiscal year does not

27 commission.

1 in the same proportion that the mileage under that individual
2 county road commission bears to the total combined mileage under
3 all county road commissions.
4 (ii) For city and village road agencies, in the same proportion
5 that the mileage under that individual city or village road agency
6 bears to the total combined mileage under all city and village road
7 agencies.
8 (7) A county road commission shall use money distributed under
9 this section only for the preservation, maintenance, routine
10 maintenance, and preventative maintenance of the county local road
11 system.
12 (8) Matching funds shall not be required from a local unit of
13 government or county road commission as a condition for the use of
14 money distributed under this section.
15 (9) As used in this section:
16 (a) "Fund" means the neighborhood road fund created in
17 subsection (1).
18 (b) "Mileage" means either of the following:
19 (i) For a county road commission, the number of miles of county
20 urban local roads under the jurisdiction of that county road
21 commission as determined under section 12b.
22 (ii) For a city or village road agency, the number of miles of
23 local roads under the jurisdiction of that city or village road
24 agency.
25 (c) "Preservation", "maintenance", "routine maintenance", and
26 "preventative maintenance" mean those terms as defined in section
27 10c.
