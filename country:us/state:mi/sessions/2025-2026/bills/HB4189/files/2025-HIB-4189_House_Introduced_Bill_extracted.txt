Section 1:
HOUSE BILL NO. 4189 A bill to amend 2001 PA 142, entitled "Michigan memorial highway act," (MCL 250.1001 to 250.2092) by adding section 1051. the people of the state of michigan enact: Sec. 1051. (1) The bridge on South Grand Avenue over highway I-96 in the village of Fowlerville shall be known as the "LCpl Michael W. Hanks Memorial Bridge". (2) Subject to section 2, a sign designating the location of the "LCpl Michael W. Hanks Memorial Bridge" must be placed at the approach of the bridge on highway I-96. (3) The state transportation department shall coordinate with the Livingston County road commission in placing an appropriate sign designating the location of the "LCpl Michael W. Hanks Memorial Bridge" at each approach of the bridge on South Grand Avenue.
