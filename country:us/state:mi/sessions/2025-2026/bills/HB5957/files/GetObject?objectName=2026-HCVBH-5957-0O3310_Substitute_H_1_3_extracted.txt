SUBSTITUTE FOR
A bill to amend 1976 PA 451, entitled
"The revised school code,"
by amending section 1531 (MCL 380.1531), as amended by 2023 PA 110.
THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF MICHIGAN ENACT:
1 Sec. 1531. (1) Except as otherwise provided in this act, the
2 superintendent of public instruction shall determine the
3 requirements for and issue all licenses, certificates, and
4 endorsements for teachers, including preprimary teachers and
5 teachers as counselors, in the public schools of this state.
6 (2) Except as otherwise provided in this act, the
7 superintendent of public instruction shall only issue a teaching
8 certificate or additional endorsement to an individual who has
9 passed appropriate available examinations.

20 teachers, school district and intermediate school district

22 department, shall promulgate rules to allow an individual to use
23 time spent engaging with local employers or technical centers
24 toward the renewal of a teaching certificate in the same manner as
25 state continuing education or professional development.
26 Enacting section 1. This amendatory act does not take effect
27 unless House Bill No. 5697 of the 103rd Legislature is enacted into
28 law.
