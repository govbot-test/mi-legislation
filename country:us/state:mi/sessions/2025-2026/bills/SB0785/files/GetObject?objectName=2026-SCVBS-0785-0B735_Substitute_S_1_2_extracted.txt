SUBSTITUTE FOR
A bill to amend 1979 PA 94, entitled
"The state school aid act of 1979,"
by amending section 30d (MCL 388.1630d), as amended by 2025 PA 15.
THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF MICHIGAN ENACT:
1 Sec. 30d. (1) From the state school aid fund money
2 appropriated in section 11, there is allocated an amount not to
3 exceed $200,000,000.00 for 2025-2026, and from the general fund
4 money appropriated in section 11, there is allocated an amount not
5 to exceed $1,600,000.00 for 2025-2026 only for the purpose of
6 making payments to participating entities to provide free school
7 lunch and breakfast to public school and nonpublic school pupils in
8 grades pre-K to 12.
9 (2) To receive funding under this section, a participating

21 the amount allocated in subsection (1) is insufficient to fully
22 reimburse districts for meals as required in this section, there is
23 appropriated from the school meals reserve fund created in section
24 30e the amount necessary to fully fund these reimbursements.
25 Enacting section 1. This amendatory act does not take effect
