Section 1:
SUBSTITUTE FOR
A bill to regulate the online sale of tickets for
entertainment events; and to prohibit the use of a bot to make
certain purchases for tickets for entertainment events.
THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF MICHIGAN ENACT:
1 Sec. 1. This act may be cited as the "event online ticket
2 sales act".
3 Sec. 3. As used in this act:
4 (a) "Bot" means an automated software application programmed
5 to perform automatic and repetitive tasks and that is designed to
6 impersonate or replicate human activity online.
7 (b) "Entertainment event" means a concert, theatrical
8 performance, sporting event, exhibition, show, or similar scheduled
9 activity held in this state that meets all of the following:
VMP S01419'25 (S-1) aef649
2
1 (i) Is open to the public.
2 (ii) Is held in a public or private venue.
3 (iii) Charges an admission fee to attend.
4 (c) "Person" means any of the following:
5 (i) An individual.
6 (ii) A sole proprietorship, partnership, association,
7 corporation, limited liability company, or other legal entity.
8 (iii) A combination of persons described in subparagraphs (i)
9 and (ii).
10 (d) "Ticket" means a physical, electronic, or other form of a
11 certificate, voucher, document, token, or other evidence indicating
12 that the person entitled to possession through purchase or
13 otherwise has a right to enter a venue for an entertainment event
14 at the date and time specified on the ticket.
15 (e) "Ticket purchasing limit" means the posted limit stating
16 the number of tickets a person may purchase for a specific
17 entertainment event during an online ticket sale.
18 Sec. 5. A person shall not use or create a bot to do any of
19 the following:
20 (a) Purchase an amount of tickets for an entertainment event
21 in excess of the ticket purchasing limit.
22 (b) Use multiple internet protocol addresses, purchaser
23 accounts, or email addresses to purchase an amount of tickets for
24 an entertainment event in excess of the ticket purchasing limit.
25 (c) Circumvent or disable an electronic queue, waiting period,
26 presale code, or other sales volume limitation system associated
27 with an online ticket sale.
28 (d) Circumvent or disable a security measure, access control
29 system, or other control or measure that is used to validate that a
VMP S01419'25 (S-1) aef649
3
1 ticket is not fraudulent.
2 Enacting section 1. This act does not take effect unless
3 Senate Bill No. 159 of the 103rd Legislature is enacted into law.
