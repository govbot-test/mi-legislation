"Michigan vehicle code,"

23 Armed Forces or in forces incorporated as part of the United States

25 eligibility criteria specified in 10 USC 1126(a)(1) to (3) for a
26 gold star lapel button.
27 (c) "Next of kin" means a person who is a qualified family
28 member of a member of the Armed Forces of the United States who
