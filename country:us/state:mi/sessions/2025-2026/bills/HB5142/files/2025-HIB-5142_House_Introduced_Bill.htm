(f) "Nonstructural interior construction" means the construction of elements that do not include any of the following:

Sec. 2023. (1) In addition to other penalties prescribed by law, an interior designer who does 1 or more of the following is subject to the penalties under article 6:

(a) Except as otherwise provided in section 2022, signs or affixes the interior designer's seal to plans, drawings, specifications, or other instruments of services that were not prepared by the interior designer or the interior designer's office, or were not under the interior designer's responsible control.

(b) Aids an unauthorized individual to practice as an interior designer, including, but not limited to, by allowing the unauthorized individual to use the interior designer's name to avoid the requirements of this article.

(2) In addition to the penalties prescribed under article 6, an interior designer who violates this article is subject to additional education or counseling.

(3) A person that engages in the practice of interior design may be found responsible for a violation of this article that is committed by an individual employed by that person.
