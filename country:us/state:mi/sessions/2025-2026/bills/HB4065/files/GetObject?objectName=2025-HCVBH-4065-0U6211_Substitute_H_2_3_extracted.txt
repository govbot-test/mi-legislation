Section 1:
SUBSTITUTE FOR
A bill to amend 1998 PA 58, entitled
"Michigan liquor control code of 1998,"
by amending section 513 (MCL 436.1513), as amended by 2024 PA 115.
THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF MICHIGAN ENACT:
1 Sec. 513. (1) The commission may issue to the governing board
2 of a college or university, without regard to the quota provisions
3 of section 531, a license to sell alcoholic liquor for consumption
4 on the premises of a conference center operated by the governing
5 board. Licenses granted under this subsection may be used only for
6 the sale of alcoholic liquor at regularly scheduled conference
7 center activities. The sale of alcoholic liquor to unscheduled
8 patrons or at unscheduled events is prohibited under this
9 subsection.
