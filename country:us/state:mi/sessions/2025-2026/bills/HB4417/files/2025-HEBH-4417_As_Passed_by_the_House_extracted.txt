Section 1:
HOUSE BILL NO. 4417 A bill to amend 1978 PA 368, entitled "Public health code," (MCL 333.1101 to 333.25211) by adding section 20911. the people of the state of michigan enact: Sec. 20911. If the department distributes an opioid antagonist, at no cost, to a person to assist the person in reducing the number of opioid-related overdoses in this state, the department shall also provide an opioid antagonist, upon request and at no cost, directly to a life support agency for the purpose of equipping a life support vehicle described in section 20919(1)(k) with an opioid antagonist.
