Section 1:
HOUSE BILL NO. 4837 A bill to amend 1978 PA 368, entitled "Public health code," (MCL 333.1101 to 333.25211) by adding sections 17771 and 17771a. the people of the state of michigan enact: Sec. 17771. A pharmacist may make ivermectin available without a prescription in such a manner as the pharmacist considers appropriate. Sec. 17771a. A pharmacist or pharmacy engaged in the business of selling drugs at retail may make ivermectin available for over-the-counter sale in oral, tablet, or topical form, if both of the following are met: (a) The drug is manufactured in accordance with good manufacturing practices. (b) The drug's packaging is clearly labeled with dosage instructions, contraindications, and safety information.
