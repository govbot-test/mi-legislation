Section 1:
SUBSTITUTE FOR
A bill to amend 1994 PA 451, entitled
"Natural resources and environmental protection act,"
(MCL 324.101 to 324.90106) by adding section 43530.
THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF MICHIGAN ENACT:
1 Sec. 43530. (1) The department may contract with a sportsmen's
2 organization to annually auction to the highest bidders up to 5
3 director's hunt licenses. Any individual may bid on a license under
4 this subsection.
5 (2) Any sportsmen's organization authorized by the department
6 to auction a license under subsection (1) may retain up to 7.5% of
7 the money raised by the auction at a rate determined by the
8 department. The remainder of the money received from the auction
9 under subsection (1) must be deposited with the state treasurer to
KHS H05875'25 (H-1) ote56i
2
1 the credit of the game and fish protection account.
2 (3) The department or commission, as appropriate, shall issue
3 any orders necessary to implement this section under section 40107
4 or 40113a.
5 (4) As used in this section:
6 (a) "Director's hunt license" includes all of the following:
7 (i) An elk hunting license.
8 (ii) A bear hunting license.
9 (iii) A spring and fall turkey hunting license.
10 (iv) An antlerless deer hunting license.
11 (v) The first pick at a managed waterfowl hunt area.
12 (b) "Sportsmen's organization" means a nonprofit organization
13 that is exempt from taxation under section 501(c)(3) or (4) of the
14 internal revenue code, 26 USC 501, regardless of whether the
15 organization is a chapter of or affiliated with a state or national
16 organization, that is organized for the purpose of conservation of
17 natural resources and the preservation or advancement of hunting,
18 fishing, or outdoor recreation.
