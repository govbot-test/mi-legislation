TITLE

An act to equalize income rights of husband and wife spouses in real estate and other property, held as tenants by the entirety.

Sec. 1. A husband and wife shall be Spouses are equally entitled to the rents, products, income, or profits, and to the control and management of real or personal property held by them as tenants by the entirety.
