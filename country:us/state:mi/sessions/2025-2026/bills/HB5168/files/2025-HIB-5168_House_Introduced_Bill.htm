HOUSE BILL NO. 5168

A bill to amend 1937 PA 94, entitled

"Use tax act,"

(MCL 205.91 to 205.111) by adding section 4mm.

the people of the state of michigan enact:

Sec. 4mm. (1) Subject to subsection (2), the storage, use, or consumption of tangible personal property sold to either of the following is exempt from the tax under this act:

(a) A person for assembly, use, or consumption in connection with a large agricultural processing facility project.

(b) A person engaged in the business of constructing, altering, repairing, or improving real estate for others in connection with a large agricultural processing facility project.

(2) Tangible personal property described in subsection (1) is exempt from the tax under this act only to the extent the tangible personal property is to be affixed to and made a structural part of, or otherwise made to be an integral part of, the agricultural processing facility to which the project relates or infrastructure improvements for the agricultural processing facility to which the project relates.

(3) As used in this section:

(a) "Agricultural processing facility" means a facility located in this state that transforms, packages, sorts, or grades livestock or livestock products, agricultural commodities, or plants or plant products, excluding forest products, into goods that are used for intermediate or final consumption, including goods for nonfood use.

(b) "Large agricultural processing facility project" or "project" means a project that meets both of the following requirements:

(i) The project is for 1 or more of the following purposes:

(A) Construction or expansion of an agricultural processing facility.

(B) Infrastructure improvements for an agricultural processing facility.

(C) Retooling of an agricultural processing facility.

(ii) The capital investment in the project is not less than $100,000,000.00.
