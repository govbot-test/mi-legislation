SUBSTITUTE FOR
A bill to amend 1949 PA 300, entitled
"Michigan vehicle code,"
by amending section 657a (MCL 257.657a), as amended by 2018 PA 139.
THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF MICHIGAN ENACT:
1 Sec. 657a. (1) A village or city having a population of fewer
2 than 30,000 65,000 individuals based upon on the 2010 most recent
3 federal decennial census may by resolution allow the operation of
4 golf carts on the streets of that village or city, subject to the
5 requirements of this section. A township having a population of
6 fewer than 30,000 65,000 individuals based upon on the 2010 most
7 recent federal decennial census may by resolution, unless
8 disapproved by the county board of commissioners under subsection
9 (3), allow the operation of golf carts on the streets of that

2 (2) If a village, city, or township allows the operation of
3 golf carts on the streets of that village, city, or township, that
4 village, city, or township may require those golf carts and the
5 operators of those golf carts to be recorded on a list maintained
6 by that village, city, or township. A village, city, or township
7 shall not charge a fee for listing golf carts or the operators of
8 those golf carts.
9 (3) A county board of commissioners may, by resolution,
10 disapprove the operation of golf carts on the streets of a township
11 located within that county if the county board of commissioners
12 conducts a hearing and determines that 1 or more of the following
13 apply:
14 (a) The operation of golf carts on the streets of that
15 township would cause significant environmental damage.
16 (b) The operation of golf carts on the streets of that
17 township would cause a significant concern of public safety.
18 (4) The county board of commissioners shall provide public
19 notice of a hearing under subsection (3) at least 45 days before
20 the hearing is conducted. The county board of commissioners shall
21 also provide written notice of a hearing under subsection (3) to
22 the township at least 45 days before the hearing is conducted.
23 (5) A person An individual shall not operate a golf cart on
24 any street unless he or she the individual is at least 16 years old
25 and is licensed to operate a motor vehicle.
26 (6) The operator of a golf cart shall comply with the signal

5 an individual shall not operate a golf cart on a state trunk line
6 highway. This subsection does not prohibit a person an individual
7 from crossing a state trunk line highway when operating a golf cart
8 on a street of a village, city, or township, using the most direct
9 line of crossing.
10 (9) The legislative body of a local unit of government having
11 a population of not more than 30,000 based on the most recent
