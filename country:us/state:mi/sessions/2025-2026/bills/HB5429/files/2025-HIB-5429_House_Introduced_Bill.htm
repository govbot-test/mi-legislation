GENERAL SECTIONS

Sec. 201. In accordance with section 30 of article IX of the state constitution of 1963, for the fiscal year ending September 30, 2026, total state spending from state sources under part 1 is $2,025,000.00 and total state spending from state sources under part 1 to be paid to local units of government is $0.00.

Sec. 202. The appropriations under this part and part 1 are subject to the management and budget act, 1984 PA 431, MCL 18.1101 to 18.1594.

The department of health and human services shall submit the reports received under this subsection to the senate and house of representatives appropriations subcommittees on health and human services, the senate and house fiscal agencies, the senate and house policy offices, and the state budget office.

(3) As used in this section:

(a) "PIHP" means an entity designated by the department of health and human services as a regional entity or a specialty prepaid inpatient health plan for Medicaid mental health services, services to individuals with developmental disabilities, and substance use disorder services. Regional entities are described in section 204b of the mental health code, 1974 PA 258, MCL 330.1204b. Specialty prepaid inpatient health plans are described in section 232b of the mental health code, 1974 PA 258, MCL 330.1232b.

(b) "PIHP region" means a region covered by a PIHP.
