Section 1:
SUBSTITUTE FOR
A bill to amend 1939 PA 280, entitled
"The social welfare act,"
(MCL 400.1 to 400.119b) by adding section 14n.
THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF MICHIGAN ENACT:
1 Sec. 14n. Not later than 1 year after the effective date of
2 the amendatory act that added this section and subject to and in
3 accordance with federal law, the department shall issue a chip-
4 enabled Michigan bridge card to a recipient of food assistance or
5 cash assistance benefits in compliance with applicable industry
6 standards as published by the Accredited Standards Committee X9.
7 The department shall continuously upgrade the Michigan bridge card
8 with any future enhanced security features that would replace or
9 complement the chip-enabling feature.
