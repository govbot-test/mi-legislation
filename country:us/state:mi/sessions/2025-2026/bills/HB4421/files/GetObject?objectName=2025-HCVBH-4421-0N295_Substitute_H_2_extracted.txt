Section 1:
SUBSTITUTE FOR
A bill to amend 1994 PA 451, entitled
"Natural resources and environmental protection act,"
(MCL 324.101 to 324.90106) by adding section 1506.
THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF MICHIGAN ENACT:
1 Sec. 1506. In the exercise or performance of his or her powers
2 or duties under this act, an agent of the department of
3 environment, Great Lakes, and energy shall not enter private
4 property without a warrant or the consent of the property owner or
5 occupant. The open fields doctrine does not apply to a search
6 conducted by an agent of the department.
