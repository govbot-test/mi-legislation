SUBSTITUTE FOR
A bill to amend 1931 PA 328, entitled
"The Michigan penal code,"
by amending section 224 (MCL 750.224), as amended by 2006 PA 401.
THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF MICHIGAN ENACT:
1 Sec. 224. (1) A person shall not manufacture, sell, offer for
2 sale, or possess any of the following:
3 (a) A machine gun or firearm that shoots or is designed to
4 shoot automatically more than 1 shot without manual reloading, by a
5 single function of the trigger.
6 (b) A muffler or silencer.
7 (c) A bomb or bombshell.
8 (d) A blackjack, slungshot, billy, metallic knuckles, sand
9 club, sand bag, or bludgeon.

2
1 (e) A device, weapon, cartridge, container, or contrivance
2 designed to render a person temporarily or permanently disabled by
3 the ejection, release, or emission of a gas or other substance.
4 (f) A bump stock.
5 (g) A multiburst trigger activator.
6 (2) A person who that violates subsection (1) is guilty of a
7 felony , punishable by imprisonment for not more than 5 years, or a
8 fine of not more than $2,500.00, or both.
9 (3) Subsection (1) does not apply to any of the following:
10 (a) A self-defense spray or foam device as defined in section
11 224d.
12 (b) A person manufacturing firearms, explosives, or munitions
13 of war by virtue of a contract with a department of the government
14 of the United States.
15 (c) A person licensed by the secretary Secretary of the
16 treasury Treasury of the United States or the secretary's
17 Secretary's delegate to manufacture, sell, or possess a machine
18 gun, or a device, weapon, cartridge, container, or contrivance
19 described in subsection (1).
20 (4) As used in this chapter: , "muffler"
21 (a) "Bump stock" means a device that allows a semiautomatic
22 firearm to shoot more than 1 shot with a single pull of the trigger
23 by harnessing the recoil energy of the semiautomatic firearm to
24 which the device is affixed so that the trigger resets and
25 continues firing without additional physical manipulation of the
26 trigger by the shooter.
27 (b) "Muffler" or "silencer" means 1 or more of the following:
28 (i) (a) A device for muffling, silencing, or deadening the
29 report of a firearm.

3
1 (ii) (b) A combination of parts, designed or redesigned, and
2 intended for use in assembling or fabricating a muffler or
3 silencer.
4 (iii) (c) A part, designed or redesigned, and intended only for
5 use in assembling or fabricating a muffler or silencer.
6 (c) "Multiburst trigger activator" means any of the following:
7 (i) A device designed or redesigned to be attached to, built
8 into, or used in conjunction with a semiautomatic firearm that
9 allows the firearm to discharge 2 or more shots in a burst by
10 activating the device.
11 (ii) A manual or power-driven trigger activating device
12 constructed and designed so that when attached to, built into, or
13 used in conjunction with a semiautomatic firearm, it increases the
14 rate of fire of that firearm.
15 (iii) A device designed or redesigned to be attached to, built
16 into, or used in conjunction with a semiautomatic firearm that
17 enhances the firing rate of a firearm to mimic rapid or automatic
18 fire.
19 (d) Multiburst trigger activator includes, but is not limited
20 to, any of the following devices:
21 (i) A device placed within the trigger guard of a firearm that
22 uses a spring to push back against the recoil of the firearm
23 causing the finger in the trigger guard to move back and forth and
24 rapidly activate the trigger.
25 (ii) A mechanical device that activates the trigger of the
26 firearm in rapid succession by turning a crank.
27 (iii) Any aftermarket trigger or trigger system that, if
28 installed, allows more than 1 round to be fired with a single
29 depression of the trigger.
