HOUSE BILL NO. 5415

A bill to amend 1984 PA 270, entitled

"Michigan strategic fund act,"

(MCL 125.2001 to 125.2094) by adding section 15.

the people of the state of michigan enact:

Sec. 15. Beginning October 1, 2027, notwithstanding any other provision of this act, the fund shall not finance or otherwise support a project or an economic development project, or provide a loan, grant, or other assistance to an entity, if doing so would violate the corporate welfare prohibition compact act.

Enacting section 1. This amendatory act does not take effect unless Senate Bill No. ____ (request no. S02934'25) or House Bill No. 5413 (request no. H02934'25) of the 103rd Legislature is enacted into law.
