SUBSTITUTE FOR
A bill to amend 2000 PA 92, entitled
"Food law,"
by amending section 7129 (MCL 289.7129).
THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF MICHIGAN ENACT:
1 Sec. 7129. (1) If a food is subject to a standard established
2 under this chapter, it shall must be identified by the name
3 required by that standard. If no standard applies, the product
4 shall must be identified by its the product's common or usual name.
5 If no common or usual name exists, the product shall must be
6 identified by an appropriately descriptive name that is not
7 misleading and that accurately identifies or describes, in as
8 simple and direct terms as possible, the basic nature of the food
9 and its the product's characterizing ingredients or properties. A
