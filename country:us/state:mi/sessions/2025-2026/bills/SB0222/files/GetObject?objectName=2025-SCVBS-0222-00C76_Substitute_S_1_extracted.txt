A bill to amend 1974 PA 258, entitled
"Mental health code,"
by amending section 473 (MCL 330.1473), as amended by 2018 PA 593.

7 individual's assisted outpatient treatment shall file a petition

9 treatment if the hospital director or supervisor believes the
