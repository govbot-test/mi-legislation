(3)聽聽No appropriations bill or conference report containing a legislatively directed spending item for which the intended recipient is a municipality or university shall be brought for a vote unless the municipality or university has submitted the following information to the House: (a)聽聽The municipality芒聙聶s or university芒聙聶s rules, policies, ordinances, and resolutions related to federal immigration law and immigration enforcement measures.聽聽This includes, but is not limited to, any rules, policies, ordinances, and resolutions regarding U.S. Immigration and Customs Enforcement, U.S. Immigration and Customs Enforcement detainer requests, and the municipality芒聙聶s or university芒聙聶s intent to work with federal authorities or actively harbor or shield illegal aliens from federal enforcement authorities or operations. (b)聽聽In the case of a municipality, an official letter from the municipality, signed by the municipality芒聙聶s chief executive officer, the municipality芒聙聶s duly elected board, or an individual who is legally authorized to act on behalf of the municipality, certifying the following: (i)聽聽The municipality芒聙聶s rules, policies, ordinances, and resolutions do not include language that requires, encourages, or supports subverting immigration enforcement in any way or refusing to comply with federal immigration enforcement measures. (ii)聽The municipality will comply with federal immigration law. (c)聽聽In the case of a university, an official letter from the university, signed by the university芒聙聶s president, the university芒聙聶s governing board, board of directors, or board of trustees, or an individual who is legally authorized to act on behalf of the university, certifying the following: (i)聽聽The university芒聙聶s rules, policies, ordinances, and resolutions do not include language that requires, encourages, or supports subverting immigration enforcement in any way or refusing to comply with federal immigration enforcement measures. (ii)聽The university will comply with federal immigration law. (4)聽聽As used in this rule: (a)聽聽芒聙聹Legislatively directed spending item芒聙聺 means an appropriation that authorizes or obligates a specific amount of money for a contract or other expenditure with a grant, loan, or other economic assistance or incentive to a specific person, organization, unit of local government, or project or activity in a unit of local government, other than through a formula-driven or competitive award process. (b)聽聽芒聙聹Municipality芒聙聺 means a county, city, village, township, or authority established under the laws of this state. (c)聽聽芒聙聹University芒聙聺 means a state university described in Article VIII, 脗搂 4, 5, or 6 of the Michigan Constitution, or an independent college or university incorporated under 1931 PA 327, MCL 450.170 to 450.177.芒聙聺.
