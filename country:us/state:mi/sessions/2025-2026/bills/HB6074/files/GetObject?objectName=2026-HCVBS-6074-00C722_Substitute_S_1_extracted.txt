SENATE SUBSTITUTE FOR
A bill to prohibit certain companies from acquiring or
purchasing certain residential property; to provide for the powers
and duties of certain state officers and entities; and to prescribe
certain penalties, civil sanctions, and remedies.
THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF MICHIGAN ENACT:

6 homes to be managed as rental properties, whether as communities
7 exclusively of renter-occupied single-family homes or as

7 of the following:
8 (a) An excepted purchase.
9 (b) The purchase of a single-family home in connection with a
10 restructuring or other reorganization of ownership of single-family
11 homes that were owned or purchased on or before the effective date
12 of this act.
13 (c) The purchase, ownership, development, rehabilitation,
14 preservation, financing, or operation of a single-family home
15 pursuant to a brownfield plan, work plan, or housing development
16 activity approved by the Michigan state housing development
17 authority under the brownfield redevelopment financing act, 1996 PA
18 381, MCL 125.2651 to 125.2670. As used in this subdivision,
19 "brownfield plan", "housing development activity", and "work plan"
20 mean those terms as defined in section 2 of the brownfield
21 redevelopment financing act, 1996 PA 381, MCL 125.2652.
22 (d) The purchase of a single-family home that is constructed,
23 rehabilitated, or otherwise designed as a dwelling unit and meets
24 both of the following qualifications:
25 (i) Is approved by the Michigan state housing authority.
26 (ii) Is rented by an income qualified household as that term is
27 defined in section 2 of the brownfield redevelopment financing act,
28 1996 PA 381, MCL 125.2652.
