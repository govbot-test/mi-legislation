Section 1:
SUBSTITUTE FOR
A bill to amend 2001 PA 142, entitled
"Michigan memorial highway act,"
(MCL 250.1001 to 250.2092) by adding section 1b.
THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF MICHIGAN ENACT:
1 Sec. 1b. A memorial designation may be made under this act
2 only for an eligible subject that is at least 1 of the following:
3 (a) A service member, a uniformed officer, or a first
4 responder who died in the line of duty.
5 (b) A military unit, military operation, military event, or
6 military award, the mission or purpose of which is directly related
7 to 1 or more service members, uniformed officers, or first
8 responders who died in the line of duty.
9 (c) An individual, group of individuals, organization, or

2
1 event that is historically or culturally significant to this state
2 or the United States.
3 (d) A mass casualty event.
4 Enacting section 1. This amendatory act does not take effect
5 unless Senate Bill No. 708 of the 103rd Legislature is enacted into
6 law.
