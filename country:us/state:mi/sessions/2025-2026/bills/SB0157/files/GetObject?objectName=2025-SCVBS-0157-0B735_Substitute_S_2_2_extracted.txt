Section 1:
SUBSTITUTE FOR
A bill to regulate pet cemeteries; to regulate the sale of pet
cemetery merchandise and services; to provide for the powers and
duties of certain local governmental officers and entities; and to
prescribe penalties and remedies.
THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF MICHIGAN ENACT:
1 Sec. 1. This act may be cited as the "pet cemetery regulation
2 act".
3 Sec. 3. As used in this act:
4 (a) "Columbarium" means a structure, room, or other space in a
5 building used, or intended to be used, for inurnment or deposit of
6 cremated pet remains.
7 (b) "Communal burial" means the interment of pet remains in a
8 grave designed to contain more than 1 pet in compliance with 1982

5 deed of certain property and restricts the property to pet cemetery
6 use.
7 (e) "Endowed care fund" means money or assets that are
8 collected or received from pet owners or others for individual
9 burials and are placed in a trust fund to be used exclusively for
10 the ongoing care, maintenance, operation, and supervision of a pet
11 cemetery.
12 (f) "Group cremation" means the communal cremation of pets in
13 a manner that does not separate the remains.
14 (g) "Individual burial" means the interment of a single pet's
15 remains in a separate grave and in a nonbiodegradable container.
16 (h) "Individual cremation" means the cremation of a single
17 pet's remains in a manner that separates the pet's remains from
18 those of other pet remains.
19 (i) "Large carnivore" means that term as defined in section 2
20 of the large carnivore act, 2000 PA 274, MCL 287.1102.
21 (j) "Local health department" means that term as defined in
22 section 1105 of the public health code, 1978 PA 368, MCL 333.1105.
23 (k) "Maintenance fee" means a fee that is collected on an
24 individual pet burial, is separate from the endowed care fund, and
25 is placed in the general fund of the pet cemetery for the care and
26 maintenance of individual pet gravesites.
27 (l) "Mausoleum" means a structure used, or intended to be used,
28 for the entombment of pet remains in a crypt or crypts.
29 (m) "Nonbiodegradable container" means a container that is

7 live in close association with human beings. Pet includes, but is
8 not limited to, a dog, cat, bird, rabbit, hamster, fish, reptile,
9 horse, and exotic animal. Pet does not include a large carnivore.
10 (p) "Pet cemetery" means any land, place, structure, facility,
11 or building provided by any person for a fee, regardless of profit,
12 to any person for use, or reservation for use, for the individual
13 interment belowground or entombment aboveground of pet remains. Pet
14 cemetery does not include land used exclusively for landfilling or
15 the communal burial of pets but does include the portion of land in
16 a pet cemetery that is used for the communal burial of pets. Pet

18 of more than 1, of the following:
19 (i) A burial ground for the earth interment of pets.
20 (ii) A mausoleum for the crypt entombment of pets.
21 (iii) A columbarium for the deposit of cremated pet remains.
22 (q) "Pet crematorium" means any land, structure, or facility
23 provided by any person for a fee, regardless of profit, to
24 veterinarians or members of the general public for individual or
25 group cremation.

6 Sec. 5. (1) Except as provided in section 33, the owner of any
7 land or a portion of land that is clear of any encumbrance and is
8 being used, or proposed to be used, as a pet cemetery shall file a
9 dedication in the office of the register of deeds of the county in
10 which the land is located. The owner shall execute the dedication
11 in the same manner and with the same effect as a conveyance of an
12 interest in land. The register of deeds shall record the dedication
13 in the record of deeds. Unless the restriction is removed, a person
14 shall not use land restricted under this section for a purpose
15 other than a pet cemetery.
16 (2) Except as provided in section 33, if pet cemetery property
17 has an encumbrance on it by a financial institution or private
18 individual whose interest is first on the deed, the owner of record

22 ceases to exist, at which time the personal representative of the

9 plat must be returned to the person and the other must be retained

11 (2) Subsection (1) does not apply to a pet cemetery that is in
12 operation on the effective date of this act.
