Section 1:
SUBSTITUTE FOR
A bill to amend 1931 PA 328, entitled
"The Michigan penal code,"
by amending section 479a (MCL 750.479a), as amended by 2012 PA 60.
THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF MICHIGAN ENACT:
1 Sec. 479a. (1) An operator of a motor vehicle or vessel who is
2 given by hand, voice, emergency light, or siren a visual or audible
3 signal by a police or conservation officer, acting in the lawful
4 performance of his or her the police officer or conservation
5 officer's duty, directing the operator to bring his or her the
6 operator's motor vehicle or vessel to a stop shall not willfully
7 fail to obey that direction by increasing the speed of the vehicle
8 or vessel, extinguishing the lights of the vehicle or vessel, or
9 otherwise attempting to flee or elude the police or conservation

1 officer. This subsection does not apply unless the police or
2 conservation officer giving the signal is in uniform and the
3 officer's vehicle or vessel is identified as an official police or
4 department of natural resources vehicle or vessel.
5 (2) Except as provided in subsection (3), (4), or (5), an
6 individual who violates subsection (1) is guilty of fourth-degree
7 fleeing and eluding, a felony punishable by imprisonment as
8 follows:
9 (a) For an individual who does not have a previous conviction
10 for fleeing and eluding under this section or section 602a of the
11 Michigan vehicle code, 1949 PA 300, MCL 257.602a, imprisonment for
12 not more than 2 years, or a fine of not more than $2,000.00 , or
13 both.
14 (b) For an individual with 1 or more previous convictions for
15 any degree of fleeing and eluding under this section or section
16 602a of the Michigan vehicle code, 1949 PA 300, MCL 257.602a,
17 imprisonment for not less than 30 days or more than 2 years and in
18 addition the individual may be ordered to pay a fine of not more
19 than $2,000.00.
20 (3) Except as provided in subsection (4) or (5), an individual
21 who violates subsection (1) is guilty of third-degree fleeing and
22 eluding, a felony punishable by imprisonment for not less than 1
