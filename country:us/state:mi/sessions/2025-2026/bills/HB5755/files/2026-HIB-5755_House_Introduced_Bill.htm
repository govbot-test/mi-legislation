HOUSE BILL NO. 5755

A bill to amend 1984 PA 431, entitled

"The management and budget act,"

(MCL 18.1101 to 18.1594) by adding section 274.

the people of the state of michigan enact:

Sec. 274. (1) The department shall issue directives requiring all state agencies to provide to the department all legislative reports.

(2) Not later than 90 days after the effective date of the amendatory act that added this section, the department shall develop and operate a website to publish the legislative reports that it receives under subsection (1). The website must meet both of the following requirements:

(a) Be accessible to the public at no cost.

(b) Be searchable.

(3) As used in this section, "legislative report" means a report that a state agency is required by law to submit to the legislature.
