Section 1:
SUBSTITUTE FOR
A bill to amend 1984 PA 431, entitled
"The management and budget act,"
(MCL 18.1101 to 18.1594) by adding section 274.
THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF MICHIGAN ENACT:
1 Sec. 274. (1) The department shall issue directives requiring
2 all state agencies to submit all legislative reports to the
3 department. A state agency shall include in its initial submission
4 to the department all legislative reports that were required during
5 the 10-year period immediately preceding the effective date of the
6 amendatory act that added this section.
7 (2) Not later than 90 days after the effective date of the
8 amendatory act that added this section, the department shall
9 develop and operate a website to publish the legislative reports
BJH H06428'26 (H-1) 4rciga
2
1 that it receives under subsection (1). The website must meet both
2 of the following requirements:
3 (a) Be accessible to the public at no cost.
4 (b) Be searchable.
5 (3) As used in this section, "legislative report" means a
6 report that a state agency is required by law to submit to the
7 legislature.
