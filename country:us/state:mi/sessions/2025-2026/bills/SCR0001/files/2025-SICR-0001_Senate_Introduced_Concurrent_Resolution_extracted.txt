Section 1:
SENATE CONCURRENT RESOLUTION NO. 1 Senator Singh offered the following concurrent resolution: A concurrent resolution granting authority for adjournment for more than 2 days. Resolved by the Senate (the House of Representatives concurring), That each house hereby grants unto the other permission to adjourn for not more than 15 intervening calendar days at such times as each house shall determine at any time during the 2025 and 2026 regular sessions.

Raw Text:
SENATE CONCURRENT RESOLUTION NO. 1 Senator Singh offered the following concurrent resolution: A concurrent resolution granting authority for adjournment for more than 2 days. Resolved by the Senate (the House of Representatives concurring), That each house hereby grants unto the other permission to adjourn for not more than 15 intervening calendar days at such times as each house shall determine at any time during the 2025 and 2026 regular sessions.
