SUBSTITUTE FOR
A bill to amend 1984 PA 431, entitled
"The management and budget act,"
by amending section 242 (MCL 18.1242), as amended by 2012 PA 430.
THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF MICHIGAN ENACT:
1 Sec. 242. (1) State agencies, community colleges, and
2 universities shall develop 5-year capital outlay plans, which shall
3 must identify capital outlay needs, including new construction, or
4 the addition, renovation, adaptive reuse, and improvement of
5 existing facilities. For state agencies, community colleges, and
6 universities, the 5-year capital outlay plans shall must also
7 include the need for special maintenance. These plans shall must be
8 submitted in electronic format to the department, to members of the
9 JCOS, and to the fiscal agencies not later than November 1 of each
