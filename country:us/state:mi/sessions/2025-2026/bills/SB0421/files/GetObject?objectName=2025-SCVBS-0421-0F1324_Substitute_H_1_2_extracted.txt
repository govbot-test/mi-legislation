Section 1:
SB-421, As Passed House, July 3, 2026
HOUSE SUBSTITUTE FOR
A bill to amend 1949 PA 300, entitled
"Michigan vehicle code,"
by amending section 811j (MCL 257.811j), as amended by 2006 PA 562.
THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF MICHIGAN ENACT:
1 Sec. 811j. (1) A fund-raising plate or collector plate
2 recognizing children's trust shall must be of a design as
3 determined by the secretary of state after consultation with the
4 state child abuse and neglect prevention children trust Michigan
5 board created in section 3 of the child abuse and neglect
6 prevention act, 1982 PA 250, MCL 722.603.
7 (2) Fund-raising donations for plates recognizing children's
8 trust collected under sections 811f and 811g shall must be
9 transferred under section 811h by the secretary of state to the

1 state treasurer, who shall credit the donation money to the
2 children's trust fund created under section 1 of 1982 PA 249, MCL
3 21.171, for the support and benefit of the children of this state.
4 Enacting section 1. This amendatory act does not take effect
5 unless all of the following bills of the 103rd Legislature are
6 enacted into law:
7 (a) Senate Bill No. 418.
8 (b) House Bill No. 4805.
9 (c) House Bill No. 4808.
