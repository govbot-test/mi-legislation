13 (2) As used in this section:
14 (a) "Immediate family member" means any of the following
15 individuals:
16 (i) An individual's parent, child, sibling, or spouse.
17 (ii) Any other individual who resides in the same household as
18 the individual.
19 (b) "Legislatively directed spending item" means that term as
20 defined in section 364.
21 (c) "Legislator" means a duly elected individual who serves in
22 either the senate or house of representatives.
23 (d) "Nonprofit corporation" means that term as defined in
24 section 108 of the nonprofit corporation act, 1982 PA 162, MCL
25 450.2108.
26 (e) "Staff member" means an individual who is currently or,
27 within the prior 2 years, has been employed by the senate or house
28 of representatives.
29 Enacting section 1. This amendatory act does not take effect
