26 that have rights to the supplier's underlying brand. As used in
27 this section, "multibranded product" means any beer, wine, mixed
28 wine drink, or mixed spirit drink that includes 2 or more brands of
29 different suppliers.

1 Sec. 1115. (1) A licensee who that elects to sell spirits or
2 mixed spirit drink between the hours of 12 noon on Sunday and 2
3 a.m. on Monday under section 1113 shall not do so until he or she
4 the licensee first obtains a permit and pays to the commission an
5 additional fee in the amount of 15% of the fee charged for the
6 issuance of his or her the licensee's license.
7 (2) The revenue received from subsection (1) for the sale of
8 spirits or mixed spirit drink between 12 noon on Sunday and 2 a.m.
9 on Monday shall must be deposited with the state treasurer in a
10 special fund to be used only by the department of public health and
11 human services in programs for the treatment of alcoholics. Any
12 other revenue resulting from the additional $160.00 license fee as
13 described in section 1114 for sales of alcoholic liquor permitted
14 under sections 1111 and 1113 shall must be deposited into the
15 general fund.
16 (3) In addition to any interest and earnings deposited under
17 this subsection, $680,000.00 of the money in the special fund
18 described in subsection (2) is transferred to and must be deposited
19 into the general fund each fiscal year.
20 (4) This section does not apply after September 30, 2029.
