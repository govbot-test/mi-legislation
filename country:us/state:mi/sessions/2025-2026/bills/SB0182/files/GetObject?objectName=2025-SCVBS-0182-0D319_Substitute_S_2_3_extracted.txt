22
23 DEPARTMENT OF HEALTH AND HUMAN SERVICES
24 Sec. 301. From the funds appropriated in part 1 for emergency
25 assistance fund, the department of health and human services shall
26 allocate $50,000,000.00 to raise the food-purchasing power of low-
27 income individuals and households. The department shall prioritize
28 payments to individuals and households experiencing the greatest
29 level of need. Funds allocated under this subsection are available
ERE S01544'25 (S-2) uf9ap5
4
1 for expenditure only if expenditure authority for federal funding
2 of food assistance program benefits appropriated in section 107 of
3 article 6 of 2025 PA 22 expires or is no longer available. The
4 department may use funding to provide for the purchase of any food
5 or beverage product intended for human consumption except alcoholic
6 beverages, tobacco, and hot foods or foods ready for immediate
7 consumption. The department may utilize existing agreements with
8 electronic benefit transfer vendors to administer the electronic
9 transfer of the funds of this state to low-income individuals or
10 households. The department shall ensure that no federal funds are
11 expended or transferred under the funding appropriated in this
12 section.
13 Sec. 302. From the funds appropriated in part 1 for Michigan
14 agricultural surplus system, the department of health and human
15 services shall allocate $10,000,000.00 for procuring and
16 distributing the Michigan agricultural surplus system to distribute
17 surplus produce to low-income residents of this state.
18 Sec. 303. (1) From the funds appropriated in part 1 for
19 emergency food bank grants, the department of health and human
20 services shall allocate funds to members of the Food Bank Council
21 of Michigan. These funds may support, but are not limited to, the
22 following activities:
23 (a) Nutrition assistance.
24 (b) Food bank resources.
25 (2) Expenditures made under this section must be made in a
26 geographically diverse manner.
