5 Michigan merit examination in grade 11, as provided in this
6 section.

7 shall include a writing component in which the pupil produces an

9 require any other extended writing sample.

11 assess a pupil's ability to apply at least reading and mathematics

23 school district, intermediate school district, or public school

24 grade.
