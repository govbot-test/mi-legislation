Section 1:
HOUSE BILL NO. 4089 A bill to amend 2001 PA 142, entitled "Michigan memorial highway act," (MCL 250.1001 to 250.2092) by adding section 16e. the people of the state of michigan enact: Sec. 16e. The portion of highway US-10 in the city of Midland between North Jefferson Road and Eastman Avenue shall be known as the "Army Specialist Levi K. Hoover Memorial Highway".
